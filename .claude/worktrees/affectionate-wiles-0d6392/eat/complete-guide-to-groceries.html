Visitor Essentials

Groceries & Bakeries

Plan ahead for your mountain getaway — local markets, bakeries, and specialty shops to keep you well-supplied.

💡

Pro Tip: There is no full-scale grocery store in Cloudcroft. Stock up in Alamogordo (20 miles) before heading up the mountain, or use the local markets for essentials.

In Cloudcroft

Local Markets & Essentials

Convenience stores and specialty shops in the village offer basics and unique local finds.

🏪

Convenience & Basics

Allsup's Market

Convenience store with gas, frozen and deli meats, milk, eggs, dry and canned goods. Also carries fresh cuts of meat.

Gas Groceries Fresh Meat

Family Dollar

Discount groceries including cooking and baking supplies, milk, eggs, bread, and toiletries. Located at 309 James Canyon Highway.

Discount Essentials Toiletries

The Weed Store

Located on Agua Chiquita Road, carrying grocery goods, diesel and unleaded fuel. A handy stop if you are coming from the south.

Gas Groceries
🥐

Bakeries & Coffee

Eight the Cake

Local bakery offering fresh baked goods, pastries, and sweet treats. A favorite stop for morning coffee and a pastry.

Bakery Pastries Coffee

Kennabelle's Bakery

Fresh-baked breads, pastries, and specialty items. Perfect for picking up treats for your cabin stay.

Fresh Bread Pastries

High Rollin' Coffee

Coffee shop with baked goods and light fare. Great spot for morning fuel before hitting the trails.

Coffee Baked Goods

Black Bear Coffee

Cozy coffee shop serving specialty drinks and light bites. A warm gathering spot for locals and visitors alike.

Coffee Specialty Drinks
✨

Specialty & Local

Club Veg at Instant Karma

Organic produce and health foods at Instant Karma, serving the community for over 10 years. A rare find for organic and specialty items in the mountains.

Organic Health Food 10+ Years

Turquoise Shop

Carries Burnett Ranch beef alongside turquoise jewelry and gifts. A unique combination of local ranch-raised meat and Southwest treasures.

Local Beef Burnett Ranch Gifts
Alamogordo

Full Grocery Shopping

For a full grocery run, head to Alamogordo — just 20 miles and 30 minutes down the mountain.

🛒

Walmart Supercenter

Alamogordo • 30 min

Full grocery selection, household items, and pharmacy. Offers delivery to some Cloudcroft addresses — check availability online.

🍎

Albertsons

1300 E 10th St, Alamogordo

Full-service grocery store with produce, deli, bakery, and pharmacy. A good option for quality groceries before heading up the mountain.

🚻

Other Alamogordo Stores

Various locations

Alamogordo also has Dollar General, Family Dollar, and various specialty shops for additional grocery and household needs.

📝

Make a List

Plan meals before your trip and stock up in Alamogordo on your way up.

❄️

Bring a Cooler

Keep perishables fresh on the drive up. Most cabins have full kitchens and fridges.

📱

Try Delivery

Walmart in Alamogordo delivers to some Cloudcroft addresses. Order ahead and skip the store.

🥐

Go Local

Don't miss the local bakeries and specialty shops for unique mountain treats and provisions.
