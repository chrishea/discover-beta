# Eighteen99

## 1. Executive summary

Eighteen99 is the current signature restaurant inside The Lodge at Cloudcroft. It replaced Rebecca’s as the Lodge’s named dining room and now serves as the property’s fine-dining anchor. The strongest current sources show it as an evening restaurant open Wednesday through Saturday, with reservations available and Executive Chef Richard Lepree publicly tied to its recent culinary reset.

## 3. What the restaurant appears to be

Eighteen99 is the Lodge’s effort to re-establish itself as a destination dining room rather than just a hotel restaurant. The official language is upscale and polished, but the practical point is simpler: this is the clearest special-occasion dining room in Cloudcroft. It appears aimed at guests looking for a slower dinner, mountain views, cocktails, and a more formal meal than the village’s casual cafés, bakeries, or barbecue spots.

## 5. Atmosphere and guest experience

The setting is one of the restaurant’s main assets. Eighteen99 sits inside The Lodge at Cloudcroft, a historic property that sells atmosphere as much as food. Based on the Lodge’s current dining page and related Lodge coverage, the guest experience likely centers on a slower, more occasion-driven dinner with a separate lounge, historic bar identity, and mountain setting.

This appears to be the best fit in Cloudcroft for:
- date nights
- anniversary or celebration dinners
- visitors staying at the Lodge
- travelers who want a “destination meal” rather than a quick dinner

Likely drawbacks:
- limited dinner days
- shorter evening service window than many city restaurants
- not the right fit for casual drop-in diners expecting a broad all-day menu

## 6. Background and history

The strongest verified history point is the rebrand itself. Chamber and Lodge sources both say the restaurant was once known as Rebecca’s and was reimagined as Eighteen99, named for the year The Lodge was established.

Recent local coverage adds context:
- In March 2024, Cloudcroft Reader reported that Chef Richard Lepree had breathed new life into Rebecca’s and described early rave reviews.
- By late 2025 and early 2026, the Lodge and Cloudcroft Reader were clearly using the 1899 / Eighteen99 identity and presenting it as a fresh chapter for the property.
- The current Lodge site is now under the 223 Collection Hotels brand rather than the older Lodge-specific domain, which suggests broader repositioning at the hotel level as well.
