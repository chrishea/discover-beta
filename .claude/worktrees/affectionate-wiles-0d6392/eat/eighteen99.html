About

The Lodge’s Signature Table

Eighteen99 is the current signature restaurant inside The Lodge at Cloudcroft. It replaced Rebecca’s as the Lodge’s named dining room and now serves as the property’s fine-dining anchor — a chef-driven restaurant with a changing dinner menu, reservation-based service, and a linked lounge.

This is the Lodge’s effort to re-establish itself as a destination dining room rather than just a hotel restaurant. The practical point is straightforward: Eighteen99 is the clearest special-occasion dining room in Cloudcroft. It’s aimed at guests looking for a slower dinner, mountain views, cocktails, and a more formal meal than the village’s casual cafés, bakeries, or barbecue spots.

Executive Chef Richard Lepree is publicly tied to the restaurant’s recent culinary reset, and local coverage frames the operation as part of a broader revival at The Lodge.

Background

From Rebecca’s to Eighteen99

A refreshed flagship dining room tied to The Lodge’s broader revival.

📜

The Rebrand

Chamber and Lodge sources both confirm the restaurant was once Rebecca’s and was reimagined as Eighteen99, named for the year The Lodge was established. The 223 Collection Hotels brand now manages the property.

👨‍🍳

Chef Lepree’s Chapter

In March 2024, local coverage reported that Chef Richard Lepree had brought new energy to the kitchen. By late 2025 and early 2026, the Eighteen99 identity was fully established as a fresh chapter for the property.

📰

Local Coverage

The Cloudcroft Reader has covered the restaurant’s evolution across multiple features, from Chef Lepree’s arrival through the rebrand and ongoing menu development.
