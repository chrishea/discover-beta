About

A Cloudcroft Spring Tradition

Mayfair is one of Cloudcroft’s longest-running spring traditions — an arts and crafts festival held each May at Zenith Park, just off the village center. It marks the unofficial start of warm-weather festival season in the Sacramento Mountains.

Expect local artisans, handmade goods, live music, and the easy mountain pace that defines Cloudcroft events. After a long winter, Mayfair is when the village steps back outside.

Full 2026 details — exact dates, vendor list, and music lineup — are typically confirmed closer to the event. Check local listings before you make the drive.
