# Cloudcroft Volunteer Fire Department

## Overview

The Cloudcroft Volunteer Fire Department is not a typical visitor attraction, but it is part of the real fabric of town life. It matters most for visitors in two ways: wildfire awareness and community events or public-safety outreach.

If you are spending time in a forested mountain village, the local fire department is relevant.

## Why this matters

In Cloudcroft, fire risk is not abstract. Visitors staying in cabins, hiking, grilling, or spending time in the forest should understand that local fire conditions can change quickly. The volunteer department is one of the clearest current local sources for community fire information and preparedness activity.

## What to know

Current official sources support:
- department website: Cloudcroft Fire & EMS
- station location: 1100 James Canyon Hwy, Cloudcroft, NM 88317
- station phone: 575-682-3473
- village Fire & EMS page links to the department
- the department remains active in 2026 public communication and FireWise outreach

Cloudcroft Reader reported in March 2026 that the Cloudcroft Volunteer Fire Department invited the public to a FireWise informational discussion, which is a useful sign of current community engagement.

## What it’s actually like

For most visitors, this is not a stop you “visit” the way you would a museum or gallery. It is more relevant as part of trip planning:
- checking current fire conditions
- understanding burn restrictions
- knowing where emergency services are based
- paying attention to local preparedness culture

On event weekends or public-education days, there may be more reason to interact with the department directly.

## Best for

- cabin renters
- summer visitors
- wildfire-conscious travelers
- people staying longer than a quick weekend
- visitors attending public safety or FireWise events

## Potential drawbacks or cautions

- This is not a regular tourism stop.
- Do not treat the station like a drop-in attraction unless there is a public event.
- Fire restrictions and burn-permit information can change quickly and should be checked close to your dates.
