# The Highland

## 1. Executive summary

The Highland appears current and is one of the newer, more ambitious retail additions to the Cloudcroft market. It combines premium gifts, New Mexico-made goods, pantry items, boots, outdoor goods, hunting and fishing licenses, and some practical mountain products. It looks like a hybrid of upscale gift store, outdoor-lifestyle shop, and New Mexico provisions store.

## 3. What the shop appears to be

The Highland looks like a broader and more contemporary retail concept than many traditional mountain-town gift stores. It seems to blend practical outdoor inventory with better gift merchandise and New Mexico-made products. That makes it one of the stronger all-around retail stops for both visitors and mountain residents.

## 5. Atmosphere and shopping experience

The public-facing identity suggests a polished but still mountain-rooted store. It likely appeals to shoppers who want something more substantial than novelty retail: boots, outdoor goods, better gifts, and regional brands. It seems like a store people use and browse.

## 6. Background and local context

Local reporting in June 2025 described The Highland as newly open on James Canyon Highway. That makes it one of the newer retail players in Cloudcroft rather than a legacy shop. Its role in the current shopping mix appears to be “upgrade the basics”: more premium gifts, regional goods, and outdoor brands in one place.
