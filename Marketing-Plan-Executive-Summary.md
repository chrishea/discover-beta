# Marketing-Plan — Executive Summary
**Mountain Monthly × DiscoverCloudcroft.com · 2026–2028**
April 24, 2026 · Companion to `Marketing-Plan.md`

---

## The bet, in one paragraph

We are not launching into a void. We start with **20,000+ combined Facebook followers** across Mountain Monthly (5,000) and the Cloudcroft Reader (15,000), and a **3,200-subscriber Cloudcroft Reader email list** with local-news-typical 40–50% open rates. That's a position most publications spend 12–18 months building. The job of marketing for the next three years is to convert that existing trust into DiscoverCloudcroft.com's owned audience, Mountain Monthly's paid subscriber base, and durable advertising revenue — without exhausting reader goodwill. Combined marketing-driven revenue: **$18K–32K Year 1, $38K–60K Year 2, $65K–110K Year 3.**

## Two properties, two lanes, one loop

| | Mountain Monthly | DiscoverCloudcroft.com |
|---|---|---|
| Mission | Community journalism, Sacramento Mountains | Trip-planning resource for Cloudcroft visitors |
| Voice | Personal, accountable, named-source reporting | Warm, knowledgeable, ground-level specific |
| One-line promise | *Community journalism at 9,000 feet* | *Everything you need to plan a Cloudcroft trip — written locally, updated constantly* |
| Primary search lane | Google News + local news SEO | Tourism keywords + AI-search citation |
| Revenue model | Ads, reader support, premium tier | Tourism advertising, lodging directory, affiliate |

Channels reinforce each other: **awareness** (social + partnerships) → **engagement** (email) → **monetization** (ads + subs) → **advocacy** (readers become ambassadors).

---

## The three discipline rules that keep this from breaking

1. **Cloudcroft Reader cross-promotion ceilings.** Max **2× per week** on the Reader Facebook page. Max **4× per year** on the Reader email list — one send per season. More than that erodes the trust that makes the Reader the launch lever in the first place.
2. **No duplicate content across the two sites.** Every high-value keyword has one domain owner. Logged in `md/keyword-map.md` before publishing.
3. **Cross-promotion is editorial, never promotional.** "Cloudcroft is getting a proper visitor guide" beats "we launched a new website." The site is introduced as the resource, not the product.

- **NM Tourism Department / NM True** — apply for co-op, pitch Cloudcroft content for newmexico.org. The state's Cloudcroft page is thin; we have local depth. The Reader's 15K-follower reach is what makes us a peer, not a petitioner.

- **The Lodge at Cloudcroft** — media partnership package (DC lodging feature + MM editorial story + dual newsletter sponsorship + in-room QR) in exchange for Lodge mentions in their channels and Founding Partner pricing.

## Three-newsletter email ecosystem

| Newsletter | Audience | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| The Mountain Briefing (MM) | Residents, regional community | Weekly, Wed 7 AM |
| Cloudcroft This Season (DC) | Visitors, trip planners | Monthly, 1st Tue 8 AM |

All three live in one Mailchimp account, separate audiences. Every new subscriber receives a 3-email welcome sequence (immediate / Day 3 / Day 7). Open-rate floor of 35% triggers immediate review.

---

## Risks worth naming up front

1. **Reader list trust erosion.** The single highest risk in this plan. The cadence ceilings are not suggestions.
2. **Velocity collapse.** The 90-day plan needs 2–3 substantial pieces per week. If we fall off, the first-mover window narrows.
3. **MM email list baseline unknown.** Day-1 audit; recalibrate Y1 target if starting figure differs from zero.
4. **Static-HTML constraints on DC.** Paid-ad landing pages, scroll-triggered popups, and BreadcrumbList schema all require deliberate engineering — none are free lifts on this stack.

---

## Top 5 actions, first 30 days

1. **Set up the three-list audience architecture in Mailchimp + audit the MM list baseline.** Half a day. Unblocks every downstream KPI.
2. **Draft and schedule the Reader launch email.** Highest-yield single marketing action of the entire plan. Expected: 300–700 new DC subscribers from one send. Plan it like a front-page story.
3. **Begin the Insider Preview** — personal emails to 25–50 stakeholders. Builds launch-day advocates.
4. **Verify GSC + Bing Webmaster Tools + GA4 against the production domain** (shared with the SEO plan's Monday-morning #2). Without it, every KPI is TBD.
5. **Submit Mountain Monthly to Google Publisher Center.** Two-to-four-week response window. Start the clock.
