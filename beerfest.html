About the Festival

Craft Beer at Altitude

The Cloudcroft Beerfest is a beloved summer tradition bringing together craft breweries from across the region for a day of tasting, food, and live music in the cool mountain air. At 8,676 feet, it is one of the highest altitude beer festivals in the Southwest.

Sample craft beers from multiple breweries, enjoy delicious food from local vendors, and soak in live music performances throughout the afternoon. This is a 21+ only event, so leave the kiddos at home and enjoy a day of mountain craft culture.

Day of Event

Event Schedule

Plan your day at the Beerfest. Gates open at noon and the fun runs through the afternoon.

12:00 PM

Gates Open

Doors open to ticket holders. Pick up your tasting glass and event wristband at the entrance.

12:30 PM

First Tasting Pours

Brewery booths begin serving. Sample craft beers from participating breweries and explore the vendor area.

1:00 PM

Live Music Begins

Local and regional musicians take the stage. Enjoy live performances throughout the afternoon while you taste.

All Day

Food Vendors

Local food vendors serve up delicious eats all afternoon. From BBQ to gourmet bites, there is something for every palate.

6:00 PM

Festival Closes

Last call for tastings and the festival wraps up. Please drink responsibly and arrange safe transportation.

Plan Your Visit

Event Details

Everything you need to know before attending the Beerfest.

🚫

21+ Only

This is a 21 and over event. Valid government-issued photo ID is required for entry. No exceptions.

🕐

Hours

Gates open at 12:00 PM. Festival runs until 6:00 PM. First tasting pours at 12:30 PM.

🎫

Tickets

Tickets available in advance and at the gate (subject to availability). VIP and general admission options available.

🚗

Getting There

Cloudcroft is 30 minutes from Alamogordo on US-82. Please drink responsibly and arrange a designated driver or ride share.
