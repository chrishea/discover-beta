About

Brewed at Altitude

Cloudcroft Brewery brings the craft beer revolution to the Sacramento Mountains with a rotating selection of handcrafted ales, lagers, and seasonal specialties. Our brewmaster uses the pure mountain water and cool climate to create exceptional brews that capture the spirit of the high country.

Pair your pint with our famous brick-oven pizzas, made fresh with local ingredients, and enjoy the relaxed mountain atmosphere of our taproom and outdoor beer garden.

Why Visit

Highlights

Three reasons Cloudcroft Brewery is a must-visit in the mountains.

🏔️

Highest Brewery

One of the highest altitude breweries in the Southwest, using pure mountain water to craft exceptional beers at 8,600 feet above sea level.

🌲

Beer Garden

Relax on our outdoor patio surrounded by pine trees and fresh mountain air. The perfect setting to enjoy a cold craft beer after a day of exploring.

🎵

Live Music

Enjoy live music on weekends featuring local and regional artists. Great tunes, great brews, and great company in the mountains.
