About

Historic Character, Real Breakfast

The Crofting has been welcoming guests since the 1990s, but the house itself goes back to 1909 — one of the oldest structures in Cloudcroft. It sits on Swallow Place, a quiet side street just a short walk from the village's main drag, surrounded by spruce trees and full of the kind of character you don't find in new construction: creaky wood floors, antique furnishings, and rooms layered with country decor and vintage objects.

The inn has eight rooms, each individually furnished and decorated — several open onto private balconies with mountain views. Mornings include a full breakfast cooked by the hosts, served at 8:30 a.m. Owners Gail and Scott draw consistent praise for their warmth and deep knowledge of the area.

This is a traditional bed and breakfast, not a resort. No kitchenettes, no late check-in, no daily housekeeping. It's a good fit for couples and travelers who want historic character over amenities.

Why Stay Here

The Crofting Experience

What guests consistently praise — and what to know before you book.

🍳

Full Cooked Breakfast

Every stay includes a real breakfast cooked by the hosts and served at 8:30 a.m. Not a continental spread — a full cooked meal. Note: early departures will miss it, as the time is fixed.

🏛️

Genuine Historic Character

Built in 1909, the house is one of Cloudcroft's oldest standing structures. Antique furnishings, creaky wood floors, and layers of vintage décor give it a feel that no modern inn can replicate.

🌴

Mountain View Balconies

Rooms 3 and 7 and the Crofting Suite each have private balconies. Rooms 3 and the Suite have mountain views — worth requesting specifically if you want to wake up to the Sacramento Mountains.

🤝

Knowledgeable Hosts

Gail and Scott are consistently cited in reviews as warm and genuinely helpful — particularly for trail recommendations, local restaurant picks, and current road and weather conditions.

💰

Competitive Rates

Starting at $119–$140/night with a full breakfast included, the Crofting offers strong value for a historic B&B. Note: a 3% surcharge applies to credit card payments.

🌿

Quiet But Central

On Swallow Place, just off the main street — close enough to walk everywhere, quiet enough to actually sleep. Lincoln National Forest trailheads are in the immediate vicinity.

What's Nearby

From 300 Swallow Place

Everything within reach of the Crofting's front door.

🛍️

Burro Avenue

Cloudcroft's main street — galleries, shops, restaurants, and the village center — is less than 0.5 miles away, an easy walk in any direction.

⛷️

Ski Cloudcroft

The southernmost ski area in the U.S. is 3 miles from the inn. A short drive up the mountain opens full winter terrain for all skill levels.

🔭

Sunspot Solar Observatory

About 20 miles south — the Sacramento Peak Solar Observatory offers tours and some of the best dark-sky viewing in the Southwest.

🏖️

White Sands National Park

About 60 miles west — a classic day trip from Cloudcroft. Early morning or sunset are the best times to visit the gypsum dunes.
