This is a fundraiser, and all proceeds will go to We Love Cloudcroft, with this tournament having a special interest in supporting Boys and Girls Bear Basketball Program.

Cloudcroft is known for relaxation and a great place to escape the summer heat!

Come play Pickleball in the cool pines. This is our second tournament on our courts so it will be great fun and for a GOOD CAUSE.

It is here! 🎉 Our 2nd Annual Pickleball in the Clouds Tournament!

This is a true FUNdraiser — come up to the cool pines, enjoy some pickleball, and support a great cause. Proceeds go to We Love Cloudcroft, a charity organization dedicated to financially supporting local youth sports.

Here’s why this event means so much: 100% of the profits go straight to Cloudcroft kids! This year’s tournament specifically supports Cloudcroft Bear Basketball (MS & HS, boys and girls). We are still accepting sponsors for this event — if you or your business is interested or willing to partner with us financially, please let me know. I will send a student your way ASAP. We also still have a few spaces available for food vendors.

A quick note for our competitive players: Our courts do have some dead spots that can affect play and cause frustration for serious competitors. We kindly ask that you keep in mind that this is a FUNdraiser for Cloudcroft‑area youth, and the spirit of the event is all about community, fun, and supporting our Bears.

Come play, cheer, and make a difference for our Cloudcroft Bears!

We will have AWESOME SWAG BAGS and food trucks available for you and your team. Please stop by the registration tent (even if you sign in on the app) and pick up your T-Shirt, which is included in the registration fee.
