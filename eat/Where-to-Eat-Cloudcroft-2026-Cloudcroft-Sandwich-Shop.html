About

A Practical Lunch Stop on Burro Avenue

Cloudcroft Sandwich Shop is a compact downtown deli built for lunch traffic, casual walk-ins, and people already spending time on Burro Avenue. It sells sandwiches, not ambiance — and that is useful in a town where many eating options lean toward barbecue, diner food, or sit-down hotel dining.

The shop fills a practical niche in Cloudcroft: quick, central, easy. Fresh subs, paninis, soup, salads, jumbo cookies, and soft-serve ice cream make it a straightforward counter-service stop for anyone looking for a non-barbecue, non-brewery daytime option.

Whether you are breaking from a Burro Avenue stroll, fueling up before a hike, or feeding a family that wants something simple, this is the kind of place that gets the job done without fuss.
