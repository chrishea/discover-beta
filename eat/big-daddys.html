About

Where Cowboy Heritage Meets Home Cooking

Big Daddy's Diner brings the spirit of the Old West to Cloudcroft with an atmosphere that celebrates cowboy heritage while striving to cook up the best grub around. This family-friendly neighborhood establishment offers a diverse menu with options to appeal to every taste—from hearty breakfast platters to satisfying dinner specials.

Whether you're a local grabbing your morning coffee or a traveler passing through the Sacramento Mountains, Big Daddy's welcomes you with good food and genuine hospitality. They also offer catering services and can host private events.
