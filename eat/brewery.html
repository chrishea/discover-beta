Practical notes

Hours, holidays & the Friday/Saturday kitchen close

Mon/Wed/Thu/Sun 11 AM – 9 PM; Fri/Sat 11 AM – 10 PM with the kitchen closing at 9 PM (bar stays open the extra hour). Closed Tuesdays. Closed Thanksgiving and Christmas Day; Christmas Eve runs 11 AM – 4 PM.

Plan a Friday or Saturday food order before 9 PM. The bar pours after that, but the kitchen is done.

Live music & karaoke schedule

Karaoke Mondays 6–9 PM. Live music Fridays 6–9 PM. Live music Saturdays 6–9 PM. Live music Sundays 1–4 PM. The Instagram account promotes specific acts — check before a music-driven trip.

Booking inquiries: booking@cloudcroftbrewing.com.

Family-friendly + service-animals-only

The official FAQ confirms family-friendly during all operating hours. Service animals are allowed inside, but there is no dog patio — pet owners hoping for a casual dog-friendly room should plan elsewhere.

Multiple indoor and outdoor spaces with tables and benches, easy to find seating that suits your group (April 2026 local input).

Parking, payment, merch & the Distillery

Parking is straightforward roadside on Burro; specific overflow guidance on event nights isn't documented. Card and cash standard. Merchandise is sold in-house only — no online merch.

The Distillery operates next door as a connected but separate space (April 2026 local input). The brewery's spirits — Mountain Smoke Whiskey, Skywater Vodka, Mountaintop Gin — are pour-and-bottle on the brewery side; ask at the counter about the Distillery's specific cocktail program and hours.
