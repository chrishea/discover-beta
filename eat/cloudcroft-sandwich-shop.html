About

A practical lunch stop on Burro Avenue

Cloudcroft Sandwich Shop is a compact downtown deli built for lunch traffic, casual walk-ins, and people already spending time on Burro Avenue. It sells sandwiches in a town where most lunch options lean toward barbecue, diner food, or sit-down hotel dining.

The shop fills a practical niche: quick, central, easy. Fresh subs, paninis, soup, salads, jumbo cookies, and soft-serve ice cream make it a straightforward counter-service stop.

Whether you’re breaking from a Burro Avenue stroll, fueling up before a hike, or feeding a family that wants something simple, this is the kind of place that gets the job done without fuss.

Why visit

Quick, central, easy

Downtown location

Right on Burro Avenue near the post office, in the center of Cloudcroft’s walkable downtown. You don’t need to drive anywhere or hunt for parking.

Fast and casual

Counter-service means no wait for a table. Order, sit, eat, and get back to your afternoon. A good fit for hikers, day-trippers, and families who need something quick.

A different option

Cloudcroft leans toward barbecue, diner food, and hotel dining. The Sandwich Shop fills the gap for anyone who wants a fresh, simple lunch.
