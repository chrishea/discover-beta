Practical notes

Hours & the Wednesday close

Posted hours are 11 AM to 4 PM every day except Wednesday. It's a lunch-only shop — no breakfast, no dinner. If you're aiming for a Wednesday Burro Avenue lunch, this isn't the stop.

Lunch rush builds between about 12 and 1:30 in the high seasons; arriving on the early or late edge of the window means a faster line.

Indoor vs. patio & the mural photo op

Indoor and outdoor seating are both available — this is more of a sit-down lunch spot than a strict grab-and-go counter. The south patio carries a Welcome to Cloudcroft mural by local artist Samantha Odom, which is a Cloudcroft selfie opportunity worth planning around.

If the weather cooperates, take a panini and a soft-serve cone to the patio and use the wall.

Inside the Burro Street Exchange

The shop is at Suite 112 inside the Burro Street Exchange — the multi-tenant building at 505 Burro Ave that also includes Burro Street Bakery, Noisy Water Winery, and Old Barrel Tea Co. Easy to combine a sandwich with a stop next door for a glass of wine, a tea blend, or a bakery pastry on the way out.

The cookie strategy

The fresh-baked jumbo cookies are repeatedly mentioned in local coverage and are the dessert run the shop is known for. The shop's hashtag — #balance — is the cue: get the sub or salad, then the cookie. Soft-serve is the optional cone on top.

Confirm before a special trip

Specific menu items, daily soup, and patio availability can shift. The official site at cloudcroftsandwich.shop and the Facebook page carry the current details. For anything time-sensitive, a quick call to (575) 551-0065 is the surest way to confirm.
