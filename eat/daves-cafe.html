Practical notes

Hours & phoning ahead

Hours shift with staffing and season. Some Wednesdays and Thursdays have been reduced or closed in recent stretches. There's no single authoritative weekly schedule — a phone call or a quick look at the Facebook page is the reliable way to check.

Facebook posts about temporary closures, specials, and live-music dates are the most current signal. Plan spontaneous visits around weekend evenings where the music rhythm tends to be steady.

Seating, payment & walkability

Bar plus booth seating — useful if you're solo at the bar, on a date in a booth, or in a small group that wants a quieter corner. The restaurant is right in the middle of walkable Burro Avenue, so parking is village-level easy and the surrounding shops, bakery, and coffee stops are all a short stroll.

Cards accepted. Tipping is standard for table and bar service.

Live music, families & the mood

Dave's is one of the Cloudcroft businesses that hosts live music through the week — the Cloudcroft Reader has noted it explicitly. Timing varies; check the Facebook page for dates. Families are welcome; this is a bar-and-grill, not a cocktail-only room, but expect bar noise on weekend nights.

Ice cream by the scoop (recently confirmed) makes Dave's a natural last stop on a Burro Avenue walk with kids.

Takeout, online ordering & what's still unconfirmed

Online ordering appears to be available through NetWaiter at davescafe1.netwaiter.com. Delivery, patio seating, and alcohol service are not fully confirmed from a strong public source — ask directly if they matter to your visit.

Editor's take
