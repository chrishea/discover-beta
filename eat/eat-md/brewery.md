# Cloudcroft Brewing Company

## 1. Executive summary

Cloudcroft Brewing Company is the village's anchor brewery-with-food — wood-fired pizza, beer brewed on site under head brewer Denver Weise, an in-house spirits program (Mountain Smoke Whiskey, Skywater Vodka, Mountaintop Gin), and a regular live-music schedule. Family-friendly, multiple indoor and outdoor seating areas (with the Distillery as a connected but separate space next door), and one of Cloudcroft's most consistently active evening rooms. Posted hours run six days a week (closed Tuesdays); food service ends at 9 PM on Friday and Saturday even though the bar stays open to 10. April 2026 menu additions: tacos, chips and guacamole, and a dessert program alongside the established pizza-soup-salad core.

## 4. What this place is

Cloudcroft Brewing Company is a true brewery-with-food, not a restaurant that happens to pour craft beer. The official site leads with on-site microbrew, wood-fired pizza is the food anchor, and Cloudcroft Reader's 2024–2025 reporting paints the business as one of the core anchors of the village's food-and-drink scene. April 2026 local input sharpens it: the pizza is consistently good (not a brewery menu afterthought), the soups deliver, the seating spans multiple indoor and outdoor spaces, and the Distillery operates next door as a connected but separate room.

In practical visitor terms: a casual brewery with a serious pizza program, a family-friendly hangout, a dependable evening stop, and one of the most useful live-music venues in town. Visitors typically stay longer than one drink.

## 9. Background and history

Cloudcroft Brewing Company has grown into one of the village's core food-and-drink anchors per Cloudcroft Reader's 2024–2025 coverage. Late-2024 expansion added more indoor seating, an additional bar, and private-event ambitions. The in-house spirits program (Mountain Smoke Whiskey, Skywater Vodka, Mountaintop Gin) gives the business a beer-plus-spirits identity and supports its take-home retail layer. Head brewer Denver Weise leads the brewing side. The Distillery operates next door as a connected but separate space (April 2026 local input).
