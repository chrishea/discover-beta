# Dave's Café

## 1. Executive summary

Dave's Café is a long-running casual restaurant at 300 Burro Avenue in Cloudcroft. Opened by David Venable in 1984 and now run by his son Chuck Venable (confirmed April 2026), it's a bar-and-grill — not a polished café — and one of the most durable local institutions on the walkable downtown strip. Burgers, breakfast, comfort food, and live music anchor the identity. Hours are variable with some midweek reductions tied to staffing, so calling first is the rule, not the exception.

## 4. What the restaurant is

Dave's Café is the sort of durable Cloudcroft institution that matters because it's steady, familiar, and woven into local habit. The feel is bar-and-grill or burger joint more than polished café — bar and booth seating, casual service, regulars mixed with visitors, and a resident live-music rhythm throughout the week. This is a locals-and-visitors crossover restaurant, not a chef-driven destination.

The building has been on Burro Avenue since David Venable opened the restaurant in 1984. His youngest son, Chuck Venable, now runs it — a point the business makes plainly on its own Facebook page and confirmed again by local input in April 2026.
