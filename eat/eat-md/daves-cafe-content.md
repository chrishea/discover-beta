# Dave's Café

## 1. Executive summary

Dave's Café is a long-running Cloudcroft casual restaurant at 300 Burro Ave. The strongest current sources show it as active, locally known, and more bar-and-grill or burger-joint in feel than polished café. Chamber listings support the current address, phone, and email. Facebook pages and local coverage suggest it also hosts live music and serves breakfast at least part of the time.

## 3. What the restaurant appears to be

Dave's Café looks like one of those durable Cloudcroft institutions that matters because it is steady, familiar, and woven into local habit. The strongest signals point to burgers, breakfast, comfort food, and a casual dining room that also functions as a live-music spot at times. This appears to be a locals-and-visitors crossover place rather than a destination restaurant built around chef identity.

## 4. Menu and specialties

## 6. Background and history

One current Facebook profile says the café was built in 1984 and that David Venable was the original owner before selling it to his youngest son, Chuck Venable. Because that detail comes from the business’s own social presence rather than a deeply reported outside source, it is useful but should still be treated as owner-supplied history. Local reporting in 2024 also notes Dave's among Cloudcroft businesses that host live music.
