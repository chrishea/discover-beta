# Cloudcroft Brewing Company

## 1. Executive summary

Cloudcroft Brewing Company looks worth prioritizing if you want one stop that covers food, beer, and a social evening in Cloudcroft. The strongest current evidence points to a family-friendly brewery and restaurant with on-site beer, wood-fired pizza, regular live music, and a location that is easy to reach from the center of town.

This does not look like a quiet, fine-dining destination. It looks more like a casual brewery with a real food program and enough event energy to matter. If you want local beer, pizza, and a place to linger, it is one of the stronger options in town. If you want a quieter dinner, a broader menu, or something more intimate, you may want another stop.

## 3. What this place appears to be

Cloudcroft Brewing Company appears to be a true brewery-with-food rather than a restaurant that merely happens to serve craft beer. The official site emphasizes on-site microbrewed beer. The menu page emphasizes wood-fired pizza, salads, soup, and a few flexible food options rather than a sprawling kitchen menu. Current local reporting suggests the place has become one of the core anchors of Cloudcroft’s food-and-drink scene.

In practical terms, it looks like:
- a casual brewery with serious pizza
- a family-friendly hangout
- a dependable evening stop
- one of the most useful live-music venues in town
- a place where visitors often stay longer than one drink

## 4. Food and drink

### What is clearly confirmed

The official menu page strongly supports a **wood-fired pizza** focus. Verified items on the current menu page include:
- Pizza Margherita
- Pepperoni Pizza
- Greek Pizza
- Zia Pie
- Cory’s Fave
- Build Your Own Pie
- House Salad
- Soup du Jour

The same menu page says:
- gluten-free crust is available for an upcharge
- vegan cheese is available for an upcharge
- soup changes daily

That makes the food program look focused rather than broad. This is not a “something for everyone” menu in the generic sense. It is a brewery menu built around pizza, a few salad and soup options, and flexibility through toppings.

### Beer and spirits

The official site supports on-site microbrewed beer. The current “On Tap Now” page says there are five extra taps that rotate constantly. Current local reporting also supports an in-house distilling program. Cloudcroft Reader reported that the business has its own Mountain Smoke Whiskey, Skywater Vodka, and Mountaintop Gin, and a 2025 Cloudcroft Reader piece said visitors can buy microbrew packs and in-house spirits there.

That suggests the drink side is broader than just beer:
- beer is clearly central
- rotating taps matter
- spirits are also part of the draw

### Does food plus drink seem to be the reason to go?

Yes. The strongest evidence suggests this is not just a “grab one beer and leave” place. The pizza, the live music, and the in-house beer all appear to work together as the core reason to visit.

### Non-beer drinkers and families

Non-beer drinkers could still find it worthwhile because:
- the food program is real
- current local reporting supports spirits as well as beer
- the official FAQ confirms it is family friendly

## 6. How to get there

Cloudcroft Brewing Company is on **1301 Burro Avenue**, on the east side of the village.

From central Cloudcroft or the main Burro Avenue shopping area:
- head east along Burro Avenue / U.S. 82
- continue through the main town stretch
- the brewery is farther east in the village along the same corridor

This looks like one of the easier food-and-drink stops to find because it sits on a primary through-route rather than tucked into a side street cluster.

What to expect on arrival:
- a straightforward roadside location
- a brewery/restaurant setting rather than a hidden destination
- likely simple access by car
- likely easy pairing with other in-town stops before or after

Less than 95% certain:
- exact parking layout and whether overflow parking is ever an issue on event nights. The location appears easy to reach, but detailed official parking guidance was not clearly posted.
