# Eight the Cake

## 1. Executive summary

Eight the Cake appears to be an active Cloudcroft bakery-coffee-art stop on Burro Avenue. The strongest current sources describe it as a business at 506A Burro Ave serving organic coffee, espresso, cinnamon rolls, pies, pastries, and custom cakes, while also functioning as an art-forward retail or display space. It reads less like a standard bakery and more like a bakery-gallery hybrid.

## 3. What the restaurant appears to be

Eight the Cake appears to sit in the useful middle ground between bakery, coffee stop, and small art space. It is not described by current sources as a large café or full restaurant. It seems better understood as a place to stop for pastries, coffee, cakes, and visual browsing while walking Burro Avenue.

The identity looks distinct for Cloudcroft because multiple articles and listings point to art in the space, not just baked goods. That makes it more of a “linger a little” stop than a purely functional grab-and-go counter.
