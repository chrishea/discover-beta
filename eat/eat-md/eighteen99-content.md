# Eighteen99

## 1. Executive summary

Eighteen99 is the chef-driven fine-dining room at The Lodge at Cloudcroft — the village's only genuine special-occasion restaurant. It replaced Rebecca's as The Lodge's named dining room and now serves as the 223 Collection Hotels property's culinary anchor. Open Wednesday through Saturday, 5–8 PM, with reservations. Executive Chef Richard Lepree leads the kitchen and, per April 2026 local confirmation, is still on-site with service and kitchen output meaningfully improved under the current operation. Prime Rib Nights and rotating special menus mean the experience varies by night.

## 4. What the restaurant is

Eighteen99 is the only chef-driven, reservation-based, white-tablecloth dinner in Cloudcroft. The name refers to 1899, the year The Lodge was founded, and the restaurant is the clearest expression of the 223 Collection Hotels group's repositioning of the property as a destination — not just a hotel that serves food. The official dining page presents it in upscale language; the practical position is simpler: when a trip calls for one real occasion meal in Cloudcroft, this is the room.

The menu is chef-driven, rotates regularly, and includes scheduled themed nights (Prime Rib Nights is the one specifically called out as a draw by locals). The experience differs by day — the kitchen is programming different menus for specific times and days rather than running a single static dinner card.

## 9. Background and history

The restaurant was originally Rebecca's, The Lodge's long-standing named dining room. It was reimagined as Eighteen99, named for 1899 — the year The Lodge at Cloudcroft was established. The 223 Collection Hotels brand took over the property's management, and the current dining page lives on the group's domain rather than the older Lodge-specific site.

Local coverage traces the evolution:

- **Cloudcroft Reader, "Fresh Offerings"** (March 2024) — reported Chef Richard Lepree's arrival at Rebecca's with early rave reviews, before the full rebrand took hold.
- **Cloudcroft Reader, "Taste of 1899's New Menu"** (December 2025) — pointed to a refreshed menu and an actively evolving kitchen.
- **Cloudcroft Reader, "A Kitchen Reborn: One Chef's Mission to Restore Fine Dining at The Lodge — and Train Who Comes Next"** (March 2026) — framed Eighteen99 as part of the property's culinary restoration.

April 2026 local confirmation: Chef Lepree is still at the stove, and service and kitchen output have both meaningfully improved under his tenure.
