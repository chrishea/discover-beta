High Rollin’ Coffee

Quick Summary

High Rollin’ Coffee is a small café-style coffee shop in Cloudcroft’s Village Plaza area, best for coffee, espresso drinks, smoothies, açaí bowls, vegetarian-friendly food, and lighter breakfast or lunch. It appears better suited to daytime visitors than dinner traffic.

Menu and Food

Confirmed or publicly listed items include espresso drinks such as latte, cappuccino, flat white, Americano, cortado, and specialty drinks including High Roller, Country Road, The Uplifter, Flyin’ High, Wild Rose, Heart Warmer, Winter Rose, Strawberry Fields, Mountain Matcha, The Healer, Taj Mahal Chai, Rosie Mountain Fog, The Saffa, Yuzu Peach Green Tea, Sparkling Fruitie, smoothies, and açaí bowls.  

New Mexico Magazine described the menu board as including grain bowls, toasts, waffles, baked goods, and smoothies, and specifically mentioned a blueberry açaí bowl with granola and nut butter.  

A Cloudcroft Reader item described High Rollin’ Coffee as offering “organic, non-GMO, vegetarian soups, toasts, and tasty bakes.”  

Atmosphere

Public descriptions point to a small, relaxed café with a courtyard feel. New Mexico Magazine described seating facing a graveled courtyard with painted wood chairs.  

What Visitors Should Know

High Rollin’ Coffee appears to be a daytime café, not a dinner spot. Hours may change, and at least one 2025 local update noted a temporary February closure, so current Facebook posts or a direct check matter before publication.
