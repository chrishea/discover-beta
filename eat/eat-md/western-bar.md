The Western Bar

Quick Summary

The Western Bar and Cafe is a long-running, family-owned restaurant and bar in central Cloudcroft. It serves breakfast, burgers, American plates, Mexican dishes, bar food, and drinks. It is best for casual meals, a full bar, mountain-town atmosphere, and later hours than most small cafés.

Basic Information

* Correct business name: Western Bar and Cafe, also listed as Western Bar & Cafe.  
* Address: 304 Burro Ave., Cloudcroft, NM 88317.  
* Phone: 575-682-2445.  
* Website: Menupix lists westernbarandcafe.com, but I did not verify an active official site from the search results.  
* Social media: Facebook page: Western Bar and Cafe. The Facebook listing describes it as a family-owned business in the center of Cloudcroft with a restaurant, bar, and related services.  
* Hours: Menupix lists Monday 7 a.m.-10 p.m.; Tuesday-Saturday 7 a.m.-2 a.m.; Sunday 7 a.m.-midnight. Restaurant.com shows similar extended hours. These should be confirmed before publication.  
* Days open: Listed as open daily.  
* Reservations: Menupix says no reservations.  
* Takeout: Restaurant.com lists dine-in and takeout.  
* Price range: Menupix marks it as “$$.” Menu items found mostly range from about $4 to $22.  

Menu and Food

The Western’s public menu is broad. Listed appetizers include chips and salsa, queso, loaded fries, wings, fried mushrooms, fried green beans, mozzarella sticks, quesadillas, onion rings, nachos, and combo platters.  

Breakfast items include huevos rancheros, Mexican eggs Benedict, chicken fried steak and eggs, steak and eggs, biscuits and gravy, French toast, pancakes, chilaquiles, omelets, and breakfast burritos.  

Lunch and dinner items include chicken fried steak, steaks, shrimp skewers, chicken Alfredo, fried cod, trout, burgers, green chile cheeseburgers, New Mexico-style burgers, patty melts, BLTs, clubs, Philly cheesesteaks, grilled chicken sandwiches, Southwest Reubens, enchiladas, fajitas, stuffed smothered burritos, tacos, tostadas, chile rellenos, carne asada, and Santa Fe chicken.  

Atmosphere

The Western reads as a casual mountain-town bar and restaurant. A menu/profile source describes pool tables, a dart board, and a digital jukebox. Menupix reviews mention dollar bills on the walls, burgers, Reuben sandwiches, jukebox music, friendly service, and a classic local bar feel.  

What Visitors Should Know

The Western appears to serve breakfast, lunch, dinner, and drinks, with longer hours than many Cloudcroft food businesses. Menupix lists it as cash only, with street parking, full bar, casual attire, good for kids, and no reservations. BringFido also says it is cash-only and pet-friendly, including an inside/patio note for well-behaved dogs. Verify cash-only status before publishing because payment policies can change.
