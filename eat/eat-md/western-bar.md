# The Western Bar and Cafe and Package Store

## Quick summary

Western Bar and Cafe and Package Store is a long-running, family-owned dive bar and restaurant on Burro Avenue in central Cloudcroft. Dark and cozy, with tasty food for lunch and dinner, beer on tap, and a full bar. Play pool or dominoes while you wait. Closed Wednesdays, open 11:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. the rest of the week.

## Menu and food

The Western's menu runs broad — classic dive-bar food alongside American plates, Mexican dishes, and a full bar.

**Appetizers**

Chips and salsa, queso, loaded fries, wings, fried mushrooms, fried green beans, mozzarella sticks, quesadillas, onion rings, nachos, combo platters.

**Lunch and dinner**

Burgers (including green chile cheeseburgers and New Mexico-style burgers), patty melts, BLTs, clubs, Philly cheesesteaks, grilled chicken sandwiches, Southwest Reubens, chicken fried steak, ribeye, New York strip steak, shrimp skewers, chicken Alfredo, fried cod, trout, fajitas, stuffed smothered burritos, tacos, tostadas, carne asada, Santa Fe chicken.

**Sides**

Sweet potato fries, tater tots, fries, onion rings, beans.

## Atmosphere

A casual mountain-town dive bar and restaurant. Pool tables, a digital jukebox, dollar bills on the walls, friendly service, and a classic local-bar feel. Play pool or dominoes while you wait.

## What visitors should know

The Western serves lunch, dinner, and drinks, with longer hours than many Cloudcroft food businesses. Menupix lists it as cash-only, with street parking, full bar, casual attire, kid-friendly, and no reservations. BringFido lists the property as pet-friendly. Call the bar directly before going if a specific item or payment policy matters.
