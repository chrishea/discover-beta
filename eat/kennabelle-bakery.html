About

The Sweetest Stop in Cloudcroft

KennaBelles Kreations Bakery is one of Cloudcroft’s core bakery stops. Located at 308 Burro Avenue, it serves both practical and pleasure roles: an early pastry-and-coffee stop for locals, a sweet stop for visitors, and a place to pick up baked goods for later.

The bakery is named after owner and baker Kenna Leonard. It looks more rooted in daily bakery trade than some of the town’s more boutique-oriented dessert businesses — locals treat a trip to KennaBelles as part of their daily routine. The Cloudcroft Reader has called it a fixture for pastry and caffeine seekers.

Whether you’re grabbing a cinnamon roll before a morning hike or stocking up on cookies for the cabin, KennaBelles is the kind of stop that’s woven into daily town life.
