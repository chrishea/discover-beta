About

Sourdough on the Mountain

Levain It Up is a small, specialized sourdough bakery in High Rolls, directly off Highway 82 on the mountain corridor between Alamogordo and Cloudcroft. It is not a café or a sit-down restaurant — it is a focused bakery stop where sourdough is the core identity.

The bakery is the work of Garrett Barnett and Steven Jackson, who have been transforming both an old building and their own lives through the business. The Cloudcroft Reader profiled them in January 2026, describing how fresh-baked bread fills the air in High Rolls on baking days.

This is one of the newer, more entrepreneurial food businesses in the greater Cloudcroft market. The limited Friday-through-Sunday afternoon hours reinforce its identity as a focused, small-batch operation rather than a broad retail bakery.

This is a grab-and-go bakery, not a sit-down café. Come in, browse the case, and take something home.
