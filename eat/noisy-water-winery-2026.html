About

A Mountain Tasting Room

Noisy Water Winery is a family-owned New Mexico winery with a tasting room right on Burro Avenue in Cloudcroft. It's part retail shop, part wine bar, and part gathering spot — the kind of place where you walk in planning to taste one wine and end up staying for an hour.

The tasting room offers over 30 wines, ranging from traditional reds and whites to distinctly New Mexican creations. Their green chile wine is the signature — the bottle people talk about, bring home as a gift, and remember long after the trip. Beyond wine, the shop carries gourmet cheeses, olive oils, balsamic vinegars, and gift items that make it double as a specialty food stop.

Even if you don't think of wine as "shopping," Noisy Water functions that way on Burro Avenue. Bottles, tastings, gift items, and the lingering pause it encourages are all part of the Cloudcroft strolling rhythm.

The Signature

Green Chile Wine

It sounds like a gimmick. It isn't. Noisy Water's green chile wine has become one of Cloudcroft's most distinctive products — the bottle that visitors bring home and that locals actually drink. It pairs perfectly with crisp fall days in the mountains, and it's the reason half the people who walk in stay longer than they planned.

What people say

"I came in skeptical about green chile wine and left with three bottles." — a common visitor refrain.

The wine works because it balances the heat of New Mexico green chile with the sweetness and body of the wine. It's not a novelty. It's a legitimate product that happens to be unlike anything you'll find outside the state.

More Than Wine

Events & Experiences

The tasting room is just the start. Noisy Water hosts events that make it more than a quick stop.

Wine Tastings

Walk in and taste your way through the lineup. The staff is knowledgeable and happy to guide you from reds to whites to the green chile signature. No appointment needed for standard tastings.

Sip & Paint Nights

Periodic sip-and-paint events combine wine with creativity. A fun evening option, especially for couples and groups looking for something beyond dinner.

Yoga & Special Events

The winery hosts yoga sessions and seasonal events like their 21+ Easter egg hunt. Check their calendar for upcoming dates — these events tend to fill up.
