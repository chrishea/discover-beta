About

What it is

Noisy Water is a New Mexico winery that started in Ruidoso and expanded into multiple tasting rooms, including Cloudcroft. The Cloudcroft location sits on Burro Avenue and functions as a tasting-room stop for New Mexico wine, gifts, and small events. The Riddle family owns the company — Jasper Riddle returned to Ruidoso in 2010 and bought controlling share in the family business, then grew it into multiple tasting rooms and a production facility in Alto. The brand’s tagline (from its own site): “We make wine, grow our grapes & take our craft seriously. But we don’t take much else seriously.”

Decision aid

Good for · skip if

Where the Cloudcroft tasting room fits in the eat-and-drink lineup.

Good for

  • Wine tasting and bottle shopping on Burro Avenue
  • Couples and groups looking for a relaxed indoor stop
  • Pre-dinner or post-lunch drinks
  • Rainy-day or bad-weather afternoons
  • Picking up a gift, a bundle, or a New Mexico wine to take home
  • Small-events nights (trivia, yoga, classes — check the calendar)

Skip if

  • You’re looking for a full meal — this is a tasting room, not a restaurant
  • You want a quiet sit when an event night is in progress
  • You came specifically for craft beer or cocktails — not the strength here
