The menu

Wood-fired pizza is the headline item, called out by the Cloudcroft Chamber as "the original wood-fired pizza." Appetizers and wings round out the food side. The exact full menu rotates by season — call (575) 682-2333 or check skicloudcroft.net before driving up.

3

Winter ski season

Typical Dec–Mar

Food service runs alongside skiing and winter tubing operations. The 2025–26 season closed March 7, 2026 because of warm weather and lack of snow.

  • Open while the hill is running
  • Pizza, appetizers, wings on offer
  • Hours follow the ski-area schedule
  • Always confirm before driving up — weather closes the hill

If the lifts are spinning, the kitchen is likely running. If the hill is closed, the food service is too.

4

  • Pizza service starts July 4
  • Runs alongside summer tubing
  • Family-friendly weekend rhythm
  • Schedule: confirm via website or social

Friday or Saturday night in summer with kids is the local move — pizza, tubing, and a slow drive home.

Where you'll find it

West of the village on U.S. Highway 82 — the corridor that climbs from Alamogordo and the Tularosa Basin up into Cloudcroft. A short drive from Cloudcroft proper, separate from the Burro Avenue village strip. You'll pass it on the way up the hill or down toward La Luz and White Sands (35 miles).

Practical notes

Hours, seasons & calling ahead

Two active windows: winter ski season (typical December through March, weather permitting) and a summer pizza window that starts July 4 alongside summer tubing. The 2025–26 ski season closed March 7, 2026, per the official site, so the hill is currently between seasons.

Food-service hours aren't published in a stable seven-day grid. Always confirm before driving up — call (575) 682-2333 or check the latest update on @SkiCloudcroft on Facebook.

Getting there from the village

The address is 1920½ U.S. Highway 82, west of Cloudcroft proper. From Burro Avenue, head west on Hwy 82 toward High Rolls and Alamogordo. It's a short drive, but it's a drive — not a village walk. You'll need a car or a ride.

Coming up from Alamogordo, you'll pass Ski Cloudcroft before you reach the village center. White Sands sits 35 miles down the hill, so a ski-hill pizza on the way back from the dunes is a real option.

What's confirmed vs. what to verify

Confirmed by the Cloudcroft Chamber directory and Cloudcroft Reader: wood-fired pizza, a summer pizza window starting July 4, and active ski plus tubing operations at the address above. The owner-supplied note adds appetizers and wings, plus the views and weekend-dinner setting.

Not confirmed in stable public sources: the full menu, day-by-day hours for the food side, alcohol service, reservations, takeout, delivery, and patio status. If any of those matter, call ahead.

Pairing with a ski or tubing day

The food service is wired into the rest of the operation. If you're skiing or tubing, food is a natural mid-day or end-of-day stop. If you're not, you're driving up specifically for the pizza, and the seasonal calendar matters more.

In summer, a Friday or Saturday evening with kids — tubing earlier, pizza after — is the pattern locals point to. Hummingbirds and elk at altitude are part of the package; Cloudcroft itself sits at 8,676 feet.
