About

A Kitchen Reborn

Step inside the kitchen at 1899 at The Lodge at Cloudcroft, where Executive Chef Richard Lepree and his team are writing the next chapter for one of the Southwest's most storied hotels. Lepree is a 34-year veteran of the culinary business who trained in Michelin kitchens across two countries. He calls The Lodge his last kitchen — and he means it as a compliment.

After decades of cooking at the highest levels, Lepree found the place where he wants to be. Now he's pouring that experience into a young brigade of Cloudcroft-grown chefs who share his passion for cooking from scratch. Together, they've built seasonal menus — most recently featuring ahi ceviche, prime New York strips, elk backstrap and mussels over handmade pasta — all prepared fresh, locally sourced when the season allows, and made with care.

This is where Cloudcroft dining reaches its highest expression: a historic setting, a serious kitchen, and food that belongs at this altitude.

The Kitchen

Meet the Brigade

A Michelin-trained executive chef leading a team of Cloudcroft-grown cooks. Every voice in this kitchen has a seat at the table.

Executive Chef

Richard Lepree

34-year culinary veteran. Trained in Michelin kitchens across two countries. Explains the difference between a cook and a chef using nothing but a tomato.

Sous Chef

Caelan Bickel

Grew up on green chile and Texas food. Now learning a whole new way to think about ingredients under Lepree's mentorship.

Chef de Partie

Bentley McNab

Handles the line with precision. Known for searing pineapple for the shrimp salad — one of the kitchen's signature touches.

Pastry Chef

Jewlyn Palmer

Keeps the line moving through what she cheerfully calls "a chaotic dance." Responsible for the dessert program and pastry work.

Also at The Lodge

More Than Dinner

1899 is the headline, but The Lodge offers several ways to eat, drink, and enjoy the property.

Thirsty Thursdays

Weekly drink specials at Saint Andrew's Lounge. A good excuse to visit The Lodge midweek when the village is quieter and the bar is relaxed.

Live Music at The Pavilion

The Lodge hosts live music on select weekends at The Pavilion. Check their calendar for upcoming acts — it's one of the few live-music venues in Cloudcroft.

Weekend Breakfast Buffet

A full breakfast buffet with an omelette bar on Saturday and Sunday mornings. One of the more substantial breakfast options in the village, especially for hotel guests.
