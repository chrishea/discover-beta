A Mountain Christmas

Christmas in Cloudcroft is exactly what it sounds like — a small mountain village at 8,676 feet, lit up for the holidays, usually with real snow on the ground. Tree lighting, a holiday market, caroling, and the kind of December atmosphere most people only see in movies.

It’s one of the easiest places in southern New Mexico to find a proper white Christmas backdrop. The whole village leans into the season.

Specific 2026 dates and the holiday event lineup to be confirmed closer to the season — check local listings.

Plan Your Visit

Visiting in December

Practical info for a holiday trip to Cloudcroft.

📍

Where

Downtown Cloudcroft, NM 88317. The village center hosts most holiday events.

📅

When

December 2026. Specific dates for tree lighting and holiday market to be confirmed closer to the season.

🧥

What to Bring

Real winter layers, waterproof boots, and chains or 4WD if a storm is in the forecast. Cloudcroft gets actual mountain winter.

⚠️

Good to Know

Holiday weekends book out fast. Reserve lodging early. Roads to Cloudcroft can close briefly during winter storms — check NMDOT conditions before driving up.
