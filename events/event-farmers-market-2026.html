About

A Mountain Saturday Tradition

The Cloudcroft Farmers Market runs Saturdays from June through September in downtown Cloudcroft. It’s a real community market — fresh produce, local honey, handmade crafts, and baked goods from people who actually live and grow in the Sacramento Mountains.

It’s an easy stop on a summer weekend in the village. Walkable, friendly, and a good way to meet locals and pick up something to take home.

Exact start dates, weekly hours, and vendor lineup for 2026 to be confirmed closer to opening day — check local listings before you go.

Plan Your Visit

Visiting the Market

Practical info for a Saturday market visit.

📍

Where

Downtown Cloudcroft, NM 88317. Walkable from anywhere in the village.

📅

When

Saturdays, June through September 2026. Exact hours and opening Saturday to be confirmed closer to the season.

👜

What to Bring

Cash (some vendors may not take cards), reusable bags, and layers — mountain mornings can be cool even in summer.

⚠️

Good to Know

Markets are weather-dependent. Afternoon thunderstorms are common in the Sacramento Mountains in summer — mornings are usually the safer bet.
