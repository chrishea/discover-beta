A Mountain Fourth of July

Cloudcroft does the Fourth of July the way mountain towns are supposed to: a parade through the village, live music, food vendors, and fireworks over the Sacramento Mountains as the sky finally goes dark at 8,676 feet.

It’s a small-town celebration in the truest sense — locals, visitors, kids on the curb, flags everywhere. If you want a Fourth that feels like it could be from another decade, this is the one.

The Day

What to Expect

A general guide to a typical Cloudcroft Fourth. Exact 2026 times and route to be confirmed closer to the event.

🇺🇸

Morning Parade

The village parade is the centerpiece of the morning. Floats, vehicles, kids, dogs, the works.

  • Route through the village center
  • Family-friendly
  • Arrive early for curb space
🎶

Music & Food

Live music and food vendors set up around the village through the afternoon.

  • Local performers
  • Food trucks and vendors
  • Family activities
🎆

Evening Fireworks

Fireworks over the mountains after dark — one of the better small-town shows in the region.

  • After sunset
  • Visible from village viewpoints
  • Bring layers — it cools off fast
ℹ️

Verify before you go. Fire restrictions in the Sacramento Mountains can affect fireworks plans some years. Confirm the parade route, start times, and fireworks status with local listings and the Village of Cloudcroft before traveling.

Plan Your Visit

Visiting on July 4th

Practical info for the holiday in Cloudcroft.

📍

Where

Downtown Cloudcroft, NM 88317. The village center is walkable end-to-end.

🚘

Parking

Parking fills up fast on busy holiday weekends. Arrive early and be prepared to walk.

🏕️

What to Bring

Layers for cool mountain evenings, sunscreen, water, a chair or blanket for the parade and fireworks.

⚠️

Good to Know

Lodging in Cloudcroft books out for the Fourth. Reserve well ahead. Confirm fireworks status — fire conditions can change plans.
