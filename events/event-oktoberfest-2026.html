About

Autumn in the Mountains

Cloudcroft Oktoberfest is the village’s annual fall celebration — craft brews, German fare, and autumn mountain colors at one of the most scenic times of year in the Sacramento Mountains. October in Cloudcroft means cool air, turning leaves, and a perfect excuse to spend a day around the brewery.

It’s a small-town take on Oktoberfest, set against pine forest and changing colors instead of a Munich beer hall. That’s the appeal.

Full 2026 dates, ticket details, and lineup to be confirmed closer to the event — check local listings and the brewery’s channels.
