# Where to Find Live Music in Cloudcroft

## Overview

Cloudcroft does have live music, but it is not a nightly, city-style music scene. The better way to think about it is this: live music shows up in a handful of venues, seasonal events, and community weekends, and the lineup changes often enough that you need to check close to your trip.

The strongest current evidence points to a few dependable places to watch:
- **Cloudcroft Brewing Company**
- **The Distillery**
- **Noisy Water Winery Cloudcroft**
- some **Lodge** special events
- Chamber and festival weekends in town

## Why this matters

Visitors often ask whether there is anything to do in Cloudcroft at night besides dinner. Live music is one of the best real answers, but only if you know where to look.

Cloudcroft’s music scene is less about one dedicated concert venue and more about:
- brewery shows
- tasting-room and bar performances
- lawn or festival music
- weekend event calendars

## What to know

### Cloudcroft Brewing Company
This is one of the strongest current live-music bets in town. Current Instagram posts show the brewery announcing lineups and bringing music back in 2026. Cloudcroft Reader’s summer guide also said Cloudcroft Brewing hosted live music every weekend all summer long in 2025.

Useful links:
- Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/cloudcroftbrewing/
- Cloudcroft Reader summer guide: https://www.cloudcroftreader.com/p/whats-going-on-in-cloudcroft-find

### The Distillery
Cloudcroft Reader’s summer guide grouped The Distillery with Cloudcroft Brewing as a regular live-music spot in summer. That is one of the clearest current local sources supporting it as a repeat music venue.

### Noisy Water Winery Cloudcroft
Noisy Water is another likely music stop, especially for smaller live sets. Public venue profiles point to live music activity there, though current published schedules appear less robust than at the brewery.

Useful link:
- Gigtown venue page: https://www.gigtown.com/venues/noisy-water-winery-cloudcroft

### The Lodge at Cloudcroft
The Lodge is not a weekly music venue in the same way the brewery is, but current local coverage shows it hosting music during special events, such as jamborees or lawn events.

### Chamber and festival events
Live music in Cloudcroft also shows up through event weekends rather than through just one venue. Current Chamber listings and Chamber social posts support a 2026 calendar that includes art shows, festivals, and Pavilion-based events where music is part of the draw.

## What it’s actually like

Cloudcroft live music is usually:
- small-scale
- close to the audience
- tied to a venue that is also serving food or drinks
- better on weekends than midweek
- more casual than ticketed-concert formal

That is part of the appeal. If you want a big-headliner venue, you are in the wrong town. If you want to sit outside with a drink, hear local or regional musicians, and have a low-pressure mountain evening, Cloudcroft can be a very good fit.

- Some live music seems strongly seasonal.
