About

Your Mountain Retreat Awaits

The Grand Cloudcroft Hotel offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and stunning Sacramento Mountain views. Opened in 2023, this locally designed, built, and managed hotel features 50 air-conditioned rooms with king-sized beds, complimentary breakfast, and thoughtful touches throughout.

Located within walking distance of Cloudcroft's finest attractions and just 15 miles from White Sands National Park, it is the ideal base for your mountain adventure or peaceful getaway. From the 24-hour snack area to the free parking, every detail has been crafted with guest comfort in mind.
