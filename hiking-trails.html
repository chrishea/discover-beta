Trail Tips

Stay safe and make the most of your high-altitude hike.

🧴

Wear sunscreen — UV is stronger at altitude.

💧

Carry extra water — you dehydrate faster above 8,000 ft.

🐻

Store food properly — bears are active in the forest.

⛈️

Start early — afternoon thunderstorms are common in summer.
