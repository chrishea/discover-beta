About

Parks & Green Spaces in the Pines

Cloudcroft may be a small mountain village, but it offers a surprising variety of parks and outdoor recreation areas right in and around town. From the bustling community hub of Zenith Park to a dedicated dog park, free pickleball courts, a scenic disc golf course, and the Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink, there is something for everyone.

These spaces are where the community comes together — for festivals, fitness, family time, and fun at 8,676 feet.

Parks & Recreation

Five unique parks and recreation areas to enjoy during your visit to Cloudcroft.

🏞️
Community Park

Zenith Park

The main community park and social heart of Cloudcroft. Zenith Park features pavilions, open green space, and serves as the host venue for the village's biggest events including the Mayfair Art Show, Beerfest, Christmas in July, and the Labor Day Hoopla. Whether you are attending a festival or simply enjoying a picnic under the pines, Zenith Park is the place to be.

📍 Central Cloudcroft 🎪 Major Event Venue 🏕️ Pavilions
🐕
Dog Park

Elevation Paw Park

Cloudcroft's dog-friendly park where four-legged friends can run and play off-leash. The fenced area features separate sections for large and small dogs, so pups of all sizes can enjoy some freedom. Bring your furry companion for fresh mountain air and exercise at nearly 9,000 feet.

🐾 Off-Leash Area 🔒 Fully Fenced 🐶 Large & Small Sections
🏓
Sports & Recreation

Pickleball Courts

Free courts located at Zenith Park, open 24/7 for players of all skill levels. Home to the Pickleball Addicts of Cloudcroft, the courts draw locals and visitors alike for games, socials, and tournaments. Bring your own paddle or join the community for some high-altitude rally action.

🕐 Open 24/7 💰 Free to Play 📍 Zenith Park

⛸️
Seasonal — Winter

Ice Skating Rink

The Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink, the Cloudcroft ice skating rink is a beloved winter tradition. Lace up your skates and glide under the pines while enjoying hot chocolate, a cozy fireplace, and stunning mountain views. A magical winter experience for families, couples, and visitors of all ages.

❄️ Seasonal (Winter) ☕ Hot Chocolate 🔥 Fireplace
🥏
Outdoor Sports

Disc Golf Course

A scenic disc golf course winding through the pines of Cloudcroft. Free to play and open to all skill levels, the course offers a fun challenge amid towering trees and mountain terrain. Bring your own discs and enjoy one of the highest-elevation disc golf experiences in the Southwest.

💰 Free to Play 🥏 Bring Your Own Discs 🌲 Scenic Pine Setting
Why Visit

Park Highlights

Four reasons Cloudcroft's parks are worth the trip.

⛰️

High-Altitude Recreation

Play at 8,676 feet in the cool mountain air of the Sacramento Mountains. Cloudcroft's parks offer a refreshing escape from the desert heat below with temperatures 20–30 degrees cooler.

🎪

Community Events

Zenith Park hosts the village's biggest gatherings including the Mayfair Art Show, Beerfest, Christmas in July, and the Labor Day Hoopla. There is always something happening.

❄️

Year-Round Activities

From pickleball and disc golf in summer to the Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink in winter, Cloudcroft's parks offer recreation in every season.

💰

Free & Accessible

All parks offer free admission and parking. Most are within walking distance of downtown Cloudcroft shops and restaurants, making them easy to fit into any visit.

Know Before You Go

Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know for a great day at Cloudcroft's parks.

🌤️

Weather & Seasons

Cloudcroft's mountain climate means cooler temperatures year-round. Summer highs are in the 70s, and winters bring snow and ice skating season. Dress in layers and be prepared for afternoon thunderstorms in summer.

🅿️

Parking & Access

All parks offer free parking and are easily accessible from the main village area. Most parks are within walking distance of downtown Cloudcroft shops and restaurants.

🐕

Pet Policy

Dogs are welcome at the Elevation Paw Park off-leash. In other park areas, pets should be kept on a leash. Please clean up after your pets and respect other visitors.
