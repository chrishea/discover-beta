# Glossary

## Brand / Publication names
- **DiscoverCloudcroft** — Tourism guide site (discovercloudcroft.com). Static HTML, no framework. This repo (`discover-beta`) is its source.
- **Cloudcroft Reader** (a.k.a. **CR**, "Reader," "voice of the mountain") — Digital publication covering Sacramento Mountains news. Domain: cloudcroftreader.com.
- **Mountain Monthly** (a.k.a. **MM**) — Print newspaper. Issues are named **issue-{month}-{yy}**, e.g. Issue 6-26 = June 2026.

## Codenames
- **Claudine** (sometimes spelled "Claudeine") — Internal codename for an AI tool/agent used in MM + CR media/social workflows. Referenced in `mountain-monthly-media-social-strategy-claudine-memo.md`.

## Entity / page shorthand on DiscoverCloudcroft
- **Poke** — A Cloudcroft business with an entity page on the site.
- **Magic** — A Cloudcroft business with an entity page on the site.
- **Cloudys** — A Cloudcroft business with an entity page on the site.
- Pattern: when Chris says "I worked on X. Live now," X is usually a per-business HTML page under `eat/`, `do/`, `shop/`, or `stay/`.

## Recurring tasks / systems
- **RSS Digest** — Automated task that aggregates feeds (Cloudcroft Reader, Village of Cloudcroft, Ruidoso News, Discover Cloudcroft) and posts a summary to Slack channel `#rss-digest` twice daily at 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM Mountain time.

## Geography
- **Cloudcroft, NM** — Mountain village in Otero County, ~9,000 ft elevation, Sacramento Mountains.
- **Sacramento Mountains** — Range in south-central New Mexico that anchors all three publications' coverage.
- **Ruidoso** — Neighboring mountain village ~40 min from Cloudcroft (over NM-244). Referenced in tourism content and RSS sources.
- **High Rolls, Mescalero, Tularosa, Alamogordo** — Other regional places that appear in Reader/MM coverage.
