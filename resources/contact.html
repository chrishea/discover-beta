FAQ

Frequently asked

A few common questions before you write in.

What can I send DiscoverCloudcroft.com?

Tips about a new business, an hours change, a closure, an opening, or a seasonal restart; corrections to anything on the site; suggestions for listings we haven't covered; and short trip-planning questions we can answer in a sentence or two.

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond within a few business days. For time-sensitive questions about your trip, the Cloudcroft Chamber has a public phone line and website listed in the Quick reference panel above.

Can I list my Cloudcroft business on the site?

Yes. Email us with the business name, address, hours, website, and a sentence on what makes it interesting. We add listings on a rolling basis and we don't accept payment for placement — listings are editorial.

I run a Cloudcroft event. Can you cover it?

Yes. Send the event name, date, location, and a registration or info link. We surface events in the seasonal guides and in the 2026 events calendar.

Who's behind the site?

DiscoverCloudcroft.com is published by Mountain Monthly LLC. We're an independent visitor guide — not affiliated with the Village of Cloudcroft, the Chamber of Commerce, or any single business.

Is this an emergency?

If you are in an emergency, call 911. The Otero County Sheriff's non-emergency line and the Cloudcroft Fire Department's number are listed above. We are an editorial site and cannot dispatch help.

Looking for something specific?

Most trip questions are answered in the section guides — try the index pages first.
