Season Overview

Peak Foliage Season

Fall in Cloudcroft is a photographer's dream. The aspen groves turn brilliant gold against the deep green of the ponderosa pines, creating a contrast that draws visitors from across the Southwest. The Sunspot Scenic Byway offers some of the most spectacular fall color viewing in New Mexico.

With comfortable daytime temperatures of 55–70°F and cool evenings perfect for bonfires, fall offers ideal conditions for hiking, scenic drives, and outdoor exploration. The summer crowds have thinned, but the beauty is at its peak. First snow often arrives in late October or November.

Fall Activities

Things to Do This Season

From leaf-peeping drives to harvest festivals, fall in Cloudcroft offers unforgettable experiences.

🍁

Fall Foliage Drives

The Sunspot Scenic Byway offers spectacular fall color viewing. Peak color typically arrives mid-to-late October with aspens ablaze in gold.

🥾

Autumn Hiking

Cool temperatures and stunning colors make fall perfect for hiking. The Rim Trail and Osha Trail pass through prime aspen groves with unforgettable views.

🍷

Wine Tasting

Warm up with local wines at Noisy Water Winery. Their famous green chile wine pairs perfectly with crisp fall days in the mountains.

🦌

Wildlife Viewing

Fall is prime time for wildlife sightings. Elk are active during the rut, and deer are frequently spotted throughout the village and surrounding forest.

📷

Photography Tours

The contrast of golden aspens against evergreen pines creates stunning photography opportunities around every corner and along every trail.

🎃

Harvest Festivals

Pumpkin patches, hayrides, costume contests, and autumn treats. Cloudcroft's Harvest Fest is a beloved annual tradition for the whole family.

What to Expect

Fall Weather

Crisp days, cool nights, and the first hints of winter at 8,676 feet.

🌤️

Sunny Days

Fall typically brings clear, sunny days perfect for outdoor activities. The famous New Mexico blue sky is at its best during the autumn months.

🧥

Cool Mornings

Mornings can be chilly, especially in late October with lows dipping into the 30s. Layer up and bring a warm jacket for early starts on the trails.

❄️

First Snow

Late October or November often brings the first snowfall of the season. Check conditions if traveling during this beautiful transition time.

Fall Events

Celebrate the Season

Harvest celebrations and community gatherings mark autumn in Cloudcroft.

October

Harvest Fest

Cloudcroft's biggest fall celebration features pumpkin patches, hayrides, costume contests, live music, and autumn treats for the whole family.

October

Aspenfest Art Walk

Local galleries open their doors for a special evening of art, wine, and fall-inspired exhibits along Burro Avenue during peak foliage.

November

Veterans Day Celebration

The community honors our veterans with a parade and ceremony in this patriotic small-town tradition in the heart of the village.

November

Thanksgiving Weekend

Escape to the mountains for a peaceful holiday. Local restaurants offer special Thanksgiving menus and the village is beautifully decorated.

Practical Tips

Plan Your Fall Visit

Everything you need for the perfect autumn mountain getaway.

🍂

Peak Foliage Timing

Peak fall color typically arrives mid-to-late October, but varies by year. Follow local reports for the best timing. Weekdays are less crowded during peak.

🧥

What to Pack

Warm layers, a jacket, and sturdy hiking shoes. Mornings can start in the 30s and warm to the 60s by afternoon. Be prepared for possible early snow.

🚗

Scenic Drives

The Sunspot Scenic Byway is the premier fall drive. Allow extra time for photo stops and be aware that Highway 82 can be icy in late November.

🏠

Book for October

October weekends are popular for foliage. Book lodging in advance, especially during Harvest Fest and Aspenfest Art Walk weekends.

Experience Fall in the Mountains

Don't miss the most colorful season in Cloudcroft. Golden aspens, crisp air, and harvest celebrations await your autumn escape.
