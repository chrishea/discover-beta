About

Secluded Mountain Living

Timberon is a private mountain community tucked away in the Sacramento Mountains, accessible via a scenic 35-minute drive from Cloudcroft along winding mountain roads. This secluded paradise offers a glimpse into authentic mountain living away from the bustle of more developed areas.

Originally developed in the 1970s as a recreational community, Timberon features a small airport, community center, and miles of private roads winding through pristine ponderosa pine and mixed conifer forests. It's a popular destination for those seeking solitude, wildlife viewing, and connection with nature.

What Makes Timberon Special

Mountain Paradise Features

Discover what makes this secluded mountain community a beloved retreat.

🌲

Pristine Wilderness

Surrounded by Lincoln National Forest, Timberon offers access to thousands of acres of unspoiled mountain wilderness with abundant wildlife and stunning natural beauty.

✈️

Private Airstrip

One of the few mountain communities with its own private airstrip, welcoming small aircraft and offering a unique way to access the mountains.

🛣️

Scenic Drive

The journey to Timberon is half the adventure, winding through forest roads with frequent wildlife sightings and breathtaking mountain vistas.

🌌

Dark Sky Haven

Minimal light pollution makes Timberon one of the best stargazing locations in New Mexico, with views of the Milky Way on clear nights.

🦌

Wildlife Abundance

Elk, mule deer, wild turkey, and countless bird species make their home here. Wildlife sightings are common and spectacular.

🏡

Cabin Rentals

Several private cabins are available for rent, offering visitors the chance to experience Timberon's unique mountain lifestyle.

Outdoor Activities

Recreation & Adventure

Endless opportunities for outdoor exploration in this mountain paradise.

🥾

Hiking

Miles of forest roads and trails perfect for exploring on foot. Discover hidden meadows, seasonal streams, and panoramic viewpoints.

🚴

Mountain Biking

Forest roads and trails offer excellent mountain biking with varied terrain and minimal traffic.

🐦

Bird Watching

Home to numerous bird species including stellar jays, wild turkeys, hawks, and various songbirds.

📷

Photography

Stunning landscapes, wildlife, and dramatic skies make Timberon a photographer's paradise.

Plan Your Visit

Visiting Timberon

While Timberon is a private community, visitors are welcome to drive through and enjoy the scenic beauty.

🏠

Private Property

Respect private property and stay on public roads. The community welcomes respectful visitors who appreciate the natural environment.

🦌

Wildlife

Drive slowly and watch for wildlife. Elk and deer frequently cross roads, especially at dawn and dusk.

🔥

Fire Safety

Fire restrictions may be in effect. Check current conditions before visiting and always practice fire safety.

📱

Cell Service

Cell service is limited throughout Timberon. Plan accordingly and download offline maps before your visit.
