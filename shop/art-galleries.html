Art Workshops & Classes

Cloudcroft Art Workshops offers world-class instruction in a stunning mountain setting. Whether you are a beginner or experienced artist, find workshops to inspire and develop your creative skills.

  • Painting workshops in various mediums
  • Drawing and sketching classes
  • Mixed media and experimental techniques
  • Plein air painting in the mountains
  • Guest instructors from across the country

Art Organizations

Join Cloudcroft's vibrant arts community through local organizations and creative initiatives.

Cloudcroft Art Society

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit supporting art programs in local schools and spearheading public art projects, including the beloved painted dumpster murals around town.

📚

Cloudcroft Library

The village library hosts art exhibitions, creative programs, and community events throughout the year. A gathering place for local artists and art lovers.

Art Events

Art Walks & Festivals

Cloudcroft hosts several art events throughout the year, bringing artists and art lovers together in the mountains.

🚶

Spring Art Walk

Stroll through Cloudcroft's galleries and shops during the spring Art Walk. Meet artists, enjoy refreshments, and discover new works as the village comes alive with creativity.

🍂

Fall Art Walk

The fall Art Walk coincides with the stunning golden aspen season. Browse galleries, enjoy live demonstrations, and soak in the autumn mountain atmosphere.

🌸

Mayfair

An annual spring celebration featuring arts and crafts vendors, local food, live entertainment, and family activities in the heart of the village.

🍁

Aspenfest

Cloudcroft's premier fall festival includes an arts and crafts fair, parade, chili cook-off, and car show surrounded by brilliant fall foliage.
