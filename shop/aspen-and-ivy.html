About

Curated Gifts on Burro Avenue

Aspen & Ivy Boutique is one of Cloudcroft’s more curated gift-and-boutique stops. Owned by Nicole Alcorn, it carries bespoke handmade gifts, original art, home goods, and beauty products — a more selective, taste-driven inventory than you will find at a standard souvenir store.

Originally located in Village Plaza, the shop relocated to Burro Avenue in 2024, settling next to the Pine Stump Mall and near The Fat Rabbit. That puts it in the heart of Cloudcroft’s walkable downtown retail district.

The boutique is part of a small retail cluster on Burro Avenue where people browse slowly rather than rush in and out. If you are looking for a gift with character or a handmade piece you will not find in chain stores, this is a good stop.
