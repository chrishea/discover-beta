Practical notes

Hours, seasons & texting ahead

The shop doesn't publish a reliable weekly schedule. In practice, expect mid-day small-boutique hours, with summer running longer than shoulder season and weekends the most consistent window.

Before making the drive up, text (512) 788-1349. The 512 area code is Austin, TX — consistent across local coverage as the owner's mobile brought from out of state, not a typo or a different shop.

The courtyard cluster

Aspen & Ivy is part of the Burro Avenue courtyard cluster that also includes The Little Gallery, The Fat Rabbit, and sits next to the Pine Stump Mall. The cluster rewards a slow browse — plan 45 minutes minimum rather than a single drop-in.

Parking is on-street along Burro Avenue. The walkable stretch runs from Black Bear Coffee at the west end past the 500-block retail to the Lodge-campus end; Aspen & Ivy sits in the middle.

Gift wrapping, returns & payment

Gift wrap, returns, and shipping policy aren't documented in current primary sources — text the shop at the number above and ask directly. If you're buying handmade or original art as a gift, it's worth confirming return policy at the counter.

Card payment is standard for Cloudcroft boutiques; cash also works. Online sales are not confirmed — treat this as an in-person shop.

Accessibility & pets

Specific accessibility details for the Burro Avenue courtyard unit aren't documented; confirm before relying on it. Small well-behaved dogs on leash are generally tolerated at walkable Burro Avenue shops, but check at the door rather than assuming.
