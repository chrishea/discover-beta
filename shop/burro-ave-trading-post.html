About

A Southwest Specialty Shop on Burro Avenue

Burro Street Trading Post is one of the core retail anchors of the Burro Street Exchange in downtown Cloudcroft. It is a specialty Southwest shop built around Native American jewelry, turquoise, sterling silver, rocks, crystals, geodes, pottery, and home décor.

This is less a souvenir stop and more a destination browse for shoppers who want jewelry, stones, and Southwest merchandise with more depth than a generic gift store. The inventory is visually specific and regionally rooted.

The store has been a fixture at the Burro Street Exchange for almost 20 years, making it one of the established pieces of Cloudcroft’s downtown shopping circuit rather than a newer retail experiment.
