What's in the shop

A themed Southwest retail room rather than a general gift shop. Jewelry and sterling silver are the headline; stones, crystals, and geodes are the distinguishing layer; Southwestern pottery and home décor round out the plate. Inventory rotates — confirm specific items by phone.

The headline category — Native American jewelry, turquoise, sterling silver.

  • Handmade Native American jewelry

Turquoise is the item that rewards a direct visit — the case depth is greater than any general gift shop can offer.

Rocks, crystals, geodes

Distinguishing layer

The stones-and-crystals program is what separates Trading Post from the neighbouring jewelry shops.

Ask to see the current geode selection — inventory rotates and the mid-sized specimens are where the interesting finds live.

Practical notes

Call (575) 682-8280 or walk the Exchange on arrival to confirm the shop is open before committing to a specific item. Suite 103 is inside the 505 Burro complex.

The Burro Street Exchange

The Burro Street Exchange at 505 Burro Ave is the indoor retail mall on the middle stretch of Burro Avenue. Trading Post shares roof space with Cloudcroft Sandwich Shop, Noisy Water Winery, Old Barrel Tea Company, and several other durable tenants. It's the best indoor-browse retail cluster in town on weather days.

Parking is on-street along Burro Avenue in front of the Exchange building. A single visit can realistically cover three or four Exchange shops without moving the car.

Authenticity, provenance & naming

Trading Post publishes both "Burro Street Trading Post" and "Burro Street Trading Co." across its own site, Chamber listing, and third-party directories. Same business, two public-facing names — not a sign of a relocation or rebrand.

On the Native American jewelry side, the shop's own statements are the strongest available documentation. If authenticity, artist origin, or stone provenance matters for a specific purchase, ask at the counter; we don't have independently verified documentation beyond what the shop itself states.

Payment, shipping & accessibility

Card and cash are standard. Online-order and shipping policies aren't cleanly crawlable from current sources — ask on the call if you're considering shipping a piece.

Exchange access is through the 505 Burro entry. Specific accessibility details for Suite 103 aren't documented; confirm before relying on it. Pets are generally not welcome inside most Exchange tenants.
