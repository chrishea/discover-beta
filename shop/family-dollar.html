About

Everyday Basics on the Mountain

Family Dollar is a chain discount store in Cloudcroft — not a locally distinctive boutique, but arguably one of the most functionally important stores in town. It covers the practical needs that the visitor-facing gift shops and galleries do not: groceries, household goods, cleaning supplies, and everyday basics.

In a small mountain town at 8,676 feet, that matters. It means locals, second-home owners, and visitors can pick up essentials without making the long drive down the mountain to Alamogordo.

This is not a curated shopping experience. It is a quick-stop store for the things you actually need, and in Cloudcroft that gives it real value.
