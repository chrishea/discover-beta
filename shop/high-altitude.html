About

Outfitter, Bike Shop, and More

High Altitude is one of the most functionally useful retail businesses in Cloudcroft: part outdoor shop, part bike shop, part clothing-and-gifts store. It serves both visitors and residents, and it belongs to the gear-and-utility side of the Burro Avenue shopping mix rather than the purely decorative-and-gift side.

The shop started in 1996 as a small bike shop opened by “the 2 Matt’s” and has grown into a three-building business. That owner-supplied history fits its current identity: an established Cloudcroft outdoor retailer with real mountain knowledge, not just a souvenir shop.

If you need trail advice, a map, a layer, bike help, or outdoor basics, High Altitude is more purposeful than most Burro Avenue stores. At the same time, it also carries boutique clothing and gifts, which broadens the appeal beyond strictly utilitarian shopping.
