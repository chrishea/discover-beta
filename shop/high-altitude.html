Practical notes

Bike rentals — read this first

Chamber materials describe High Altitude as doing bike rentals. The shop's own current bicycles page says it's not offering rentals for the season. Policy shifts — but if rentals matter to your trip, call (575) 682-1229 before planning around them. Don't trust third-party directories on this one.

Hours & seasonal rhythm

Mon–Thu 10–5:30 · Fri–Sat 10–6 · Sun 10–5, per the official site. These are posted and consistent. Seasonal adjustments happen; a quick call is still cheap insurance before a timed trip.

Maps & staff

Free local trail maps at the counter plus maps available for purchase — this is Cloudcroft's best on-foot map supplier. Staff are informed and will sketch out trail routes if you ask.

Parking, payment & accessibility

On-street parking along Burro Avenue. Card and cash accepted. Specific accessibility details for the 310 Burro unit aren't documented; confirm before relying on it.
