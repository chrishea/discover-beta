Practical notes

Hours & class schedule

Shop floor is open daily 12–5 per the official site. Yoga classes run on a separate schedule with temporary no-class windows — check getinstantkarma.com before a timed visit. Chai-bar hours likely track the shop floor but aren't separately documented.

The hybrid identity — what to expect

Instant Karma is a wellness business with a storefront, not a shop with wellness on the side. The best way in for a visitor is a chai plus a slow boutique browse, optionally paired with a class booking if your trip allows. Don't expect a quick-browse pattern — the shop rewards sitting down.

Club Veg & community programs

The Club Veg organic-produce pickup program operates out of the same space. Availability varies by week and season; visitors aren't the primary audience but locals can ask directly about current membership and bags.

Payment, parking & accessibility

Card and cash standard. Dedicated Square shop for online orders. Parking on-street along Burro. Specific accessibility details for 302 Burro aren't documented; ask directly if it matters.

Editor's take

"Every town thinks it has a yoga studio and a gift shop. Few towns have one person running both plus a chai bar and an organic-produce pickup, at the same address. Instant Karma is Cloudcroft's most genuinely editorial retail stop — and the one where the honest review is simply: sit down, order the chai, and let the place explain itself."
