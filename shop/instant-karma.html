About

Where Good Energy Lives

Instant Karma has been Cloudcroft's beloved destination for unique gifts and spiritual treasures since 2005. Step inside and discover a carefully curated collection of crystals, healing stones, handcrafted jewelry, incense, candles, and eclectic decor from artisans worldwide.

Whether you're seeking the perfect gift, a meaningful keepsake, or simply good vibes, our welcoming shop offers something special for every soul who walks through our doors. Instant Karma is also home to Club Veg, providing organic produce to the Cloudcroft community for over 10 years.
