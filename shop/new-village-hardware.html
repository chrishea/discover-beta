About

The Hardware Store That Keeps You on the Mountain

New Village Hardware is one of the most practically important retail businesses in Cloudcroft. In a shopping district where many stores are oriented toward visitors, this one is clearly local-serving — stocking plumbing, electrical supplies, paint, wood pellets, and other mountain-life essentials.

The store fills a critical niche: it keeps residents, cabin owners, and contractors from having to drive down the mountain for basic repair and home-maintenance supplies. Whether you need a pipe fitting, a gallon of paint, or a bag of pellets for winter, this is the place.

For visitors with cabin or RV needs, New Village Hardware is equally useful. It is the kind of store you are glad exists when something breaks at 8,676 feet.
