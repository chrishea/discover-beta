Practical notes

Hours & same-day confirmation

Mon–Sat 7–5, Sun 10–4 appears consistently across Waze and other current listings. Not posted on a single authoritative site — a quick call to (575) 682-2849 before a timed trip is reasonable, especially on Sundays or holidays.

Services to confirm by phone

Paint matching / tinting, key cutting, propane exchange or refill, tool rentals, contractor account pricing, delivery or pickup, and formal special-order programs are all plausible for a store of this type but not confirmed by a primary source. Call first if any of these would decide your trip.

Address conflicts (Curlew vs JCH)

Some older directory pages still show a James Canyon Highway address. Current Waze and map sources all use 61 Curlew Place — treat that as authoritative. If in doubt, call ahead.

Parking, payment & accessibility

Parking on-site. Card and cash standard. Specific accessibility details for the Curlew Place building aren't documented; confirm before relying on it.

Editor's take

A page exists here for completeness, not because you're meant to drive up to browse it."
