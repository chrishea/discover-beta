About

The Store That Keeps You On the Mountain

New Village Hardware is the working hardware store of Cloudcroft. It is not a gift boutique or a crafts stop. It is the place residents, cabin owners, contractors, and visitors with mountain-life needs go for plumbing, electrical, paint, lumber, tools, and wood pellets. In a village where most retail leans toward souvenirs and galleries, this one plays the opposite role.

The store runs seven days a week with early 7:00 a.m. weekday openings — hours that match contractors and early-start home projects more than browsing tourists. Customer reviews describe a family-run operation with friendly, knowledgeable staff and a willingness to track down items the store does not keep in stock.

For Cloudcroft households and cabin owners, New Village Hardware is the difference between solving a problem at 7:15 a.m. and losing half a day driving to Alamogordo for a $6 plumbing fitting.
