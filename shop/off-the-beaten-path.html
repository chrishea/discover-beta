About

Art You Will Not Find Elsewhere

Off the Beaten Path is one of Cloudcroft’s most distinctive art-and-gift shops. It looks more gallery-like than many other stores in town, with a strong focus on original artwork, eclectic gifts, sculpture, metalwork, ceramics, and handmade work from local, New Mexico, and national artists.

Founded in 1996, the shop has built its identity around curated, unusual finds rather than standard tourist merchandise. Much of the work is created from discarded scraps and found items, reinforcing a repurposed-art identity that sets it apart.

The pink-and-turquoise gallery includes a patio and sculpture garden, making the browsing experience slower, more visual, and more gallery-like than at most Burro Avenue retail stops.

Why Visit

Worth Seeking Out

🎨

Gallery, Not Gift Shop

The environment matters here almost as much as the merchandise. A patio, sculpture garden, and pink-and-turquoise exterior make this a visual experience, not just a retail stop.

♻️

Repurposed Art Identity

Much of the work is created from discarded scraps and found items. That gives the shop a creative philosophy that goes beyond simply stocking products.

📅

Established Since 1996

Nearly three decades in Cloudcroft. That longevity makes Off the Beaten Path one of the more established art-oriented stops in the village.
