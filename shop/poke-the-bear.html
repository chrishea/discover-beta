What's in the shop

Inventory rotates constantly — this is the place to walk in, browse, and let the shop show you what's there today.

1

Humor & novelty

House specialty

The aisle that sets the tone — gag gifts, novelty toys, and the kind of stocking-stuffer pickups you didn't plan to bring home.

  • Magic 8 Balls, Rubik's cubes
  • Novelty keychains
  • Small action figurines
  • Water toys, gag gifts

If you came in with kids or for a road-trip gift, this is the wall to hit first.

2

Stickers, souvenirs & Bigfoot

Personality pickup

Cloudcroft-themed merch and an honest-to-goodness Bigfoot section — the shop's sense of humor in retail form.

  • Sticker spinners
  • Coasters, magnets, patches
  • Bigfoot items
  • Cloudcroft-themed gifts

The Bigfoot shelf is worth a look even if you don't buy — it tells you what kind of shop this is.

3

Apparel, plush & outdoor

Mountain-town basics with a sense of humor — printed tees, hoodies, animal-print hats, plush, blankets, and outdoor odds-and-ends.

  • T-shirts & hoodies
  • Hats including Lazy One animal hats
  • Plush toys & boa-feather pieces
  • Blankets & outdoor gear

A printed hoodie or a plush oddity that travels home as a story, not a generic souvenir.

4

Vintage & antiques (back room)

A back-room layer for shoppers who like older finds — framed art, lamps, upcycled furniture, and one-off collectibles.

  • Framed prints & art
  • Lamps & small upcycled furniture
  • Antiques, collectibles & curios
  • Hand-crafted textiles

Ask at the counter about a piece you're curious about — the story usually comes free.

Decision aid

Good for · skip if

Good for

  • Shoppers who want something for everyone in one stop
  • Humor gifts, novelty toys, and stocking-stuffer pickups
  • Cloudcroft-themed stickers, magnets, and Bigfoot souvenirs
  • Mountain apparel, plush, and gag-gift energy
  • A slow browse with a vintage back room as a bonus
  • Visitors with dogs — the porch is dog-welcome

Skip if

  • You need guaranteed hours — call first
  • You want curated fine art — go to Off the Beaten Path
  • You want clothing-first fashion — go to Coco Blu
  • You want a specific piece — this isn't a fixed-inventory shop

Practical notes

Hours & calling ahead

No posted weekly schedule available in current sources. Call (575) 682-1341 or email ptbear510@yahoo.com before timing a visit.

Inventory rotation & specifics

An eclectic shop rotates inventory constantly. Photos on this page represent the shop's character, not a catalog of current stock — don't plan around a specific piece.

Dogs on the porch

The porch carries a "Your dog is welcome" sign — bring the dog along on the Burro Avenue walk. Standard small-shop courtesy applies inside.

What's not confirmed

Current social accounts and any e-commerce / shipping policies aren't confirmed in current sources. The Chamber page and Explore Cloudcroft listing confirm the business plus the phone, email, and 510 Burro address.

Parking, payment & accessibility

On-street parking along Burro. Card and cash standard (typical small-shop assumption). Specific accessibility details aren't documented.

Editor's take
