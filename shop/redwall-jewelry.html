What's in the shop

Handmade jewelry in a defined material mix. The online shop exists but only reflects a taste of the in-store range; specific makers and service offerings rotate.

1

Silver & gold

Core materials

Sterling silver and gold — the shop's foundational metals.

  • Sterling silver rings, cuffs, earrings
  • Gold pieces
  • Handmade in-house (mix with curated makers — confirm)

Turquoise & opal

The stones that carry the Cloudcroft and Southwest resonance, done in a handmade-jewelry context.

Practical notes

Hours & calling ahead

No posted weekly schedule in current sources. Call (575) 682-4414 or email redwallfinejewelry@gmail.com before timing a visit. Exchange traffic tends to be steadiest on summer weekends.

Handmade vs. curated

Official-site language is "unique handmade pieces in every price range." That suggests an in-house handmade focus, but the balance between handmade in-house and curated-from-outside-makers isn't documented. Ask at the counter if the distinction matters for your purchase.

Repair, resize & custom work

Repair, resizing, and custom-work services are plausible for a store of this age and specialty, but aren't confirmed in current crawlable sources. Call before planning a visit around a specific service.

Parking, payment & accessibility

On-street parking along Burro. Card and cash standard. Exchange access is through the 505 Burro entry; specific accessibility for Suite 107 isn't documented.
