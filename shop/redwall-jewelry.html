Practical notes

Hours

Open seven days a week, 10 AM – 5 PM. Same schedule every day. The "Open now" pill at the top of the page reflects today's status.

The Burro Street Exchange itinerary

RedWall is one of three tenants in the same 505 Burro Ave building. The natural visit sequence: grab a coffee at the Burro Street Bakery in Suite 106, shop RedWall in Suite 107, and walk yards to the Cloudcroft Sandwich Shop in Suite 112 for lunch. Three stops, one building, an easy half-morning.

Handmade vs. curated

Official-site language is "unique handmade pieces in every price range." That suggests an in-house handmade focus, but the balance between handmade in-house and curated-from-outside-makers isn't documented. Ask at the counter if the distinction matters for your purchase.

Repair, resize & custom work

Repair, resizing, and custom-work services are plausible for a store of this age and specialty, but aren't confirmed in current crawlable sources. Call before planning a visit around a specific service.

Parking, payment & accessibility

On-street parking along Burro. Card and cash standard. Exchange access is through the 505 Burro entry; specific accessibility for Suite 107 isn't documented.

Editor's take

Fifty years in tells you it's doing something right. Don't walk in expecting a fast browse; expect a real jewelry conversation."
