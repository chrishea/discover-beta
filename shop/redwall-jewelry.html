About

A Jewelry Anchor on Burro Avenue

RedWall Jewelry is one of Cloudcroft’s more distinctive specialty-retail stops: locally owned, jewelry-focused, and built around gemstone, turquoise, opal, diamond, sterling-silver, and gold pieces. Its identity is not souvenir retail but fine or semi-fine jewelry with a mountain-village setting.

Established in Cloudcroft since the 1970s, RedWall has served as one of the core jewelry anchors on Burro Avenue for decades. The official site presents themed collections including color, luxury, minimal, nature, and turquoise lines.

This is a browse-slowly store rather than a quick in-and-out souvenir stop. Cases, collections, and gemstone-specific inventory encourage comparison shopping for shoppers who want jewelry with regional texture or a gift with permanence.
