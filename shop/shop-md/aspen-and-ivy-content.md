## 3. What the shop appears to be

Aspen & Ivy appears to be one of Cloudcroft’s more curated gift-and-boutique stops. It looks less like a clothing-heavy boutique than CoCo Blu and less like a general souvenir store than some other Burro Avenue shops. Current local coverage positions it as a source for handmade gifts, local art, and home goods, which suggests a more selective, taste-driven inventory.

## 6. Background and local context

Cloudcroft Reader reported in March 2024 that Aspen & Ivy was in Village Plaza and carried home goods, beauty products, bespoke gifts, and more. The same outlet later reported in August 2024 that Nicole Alcorn’s Aspen & Ivy relocated from the Village Plaza to Burro Street next to the Pine Stump Mall. Later 2024 and 2025 coverage continues to place it on Burro Avenue and identifies Nicole Alcorn as the owner.

That makes Aspen & Ivy a useful example of a newer or evolving retail business rather than a long-established legacy shop.
