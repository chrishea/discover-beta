# Coco Blu

## 1. Executive summary

Coco Blu is Cloudcroft's clothing-led women's boutique — a full-look fashion shop at 94 Little Glorietta Ave, just off the main Burro Avenue strip, covering clothing, shoes, jewelry, handbags, beauty, accessories, and home accents under a single "From Head to Tootsies" positioning. Founded by Carolina Byers in May 2018, Coco Blu is one of the cleaner-documented shops in town — the official site posts a consistent weekly schedule, cleanly lists contact details, and presents the business as a fashion boutique rather than a mountain-gear store or general gift shop. That editorial clarity is rare on the Cloudcroft retail map and makes this a reliable stop to build a trip around.

## 4. What the shop is

Coco Blu is the clothing-forward women's boutique on the Cloudcroft retail map. The official site is unusually clear for Cloudcroft retail: it names the founder, dates the opening, lists the merchandise mix, and posts a weekly schedule. The positioning — "From Head to Tootsies" — is doing the editorial work of framing the shop as a full-look stop rather than a single-category room: clothing drives the visit, but shoes, jewelry, handbags, beauty, accessories, and home accents all round out the inventory.

That category breadth separates Coco Blu from Cloudcroft's other women-leaning retail. Aspen &amp; Ivy is curated-gift/handmade/art; Instant Karma is fair-trade-yoga-lifestyle; Coco Blu is the fashion boutique — the shop to stop at when what you want is clothing first.
