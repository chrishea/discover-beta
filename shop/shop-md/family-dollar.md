## 4. What the store is

Family Dollar in Cloudcroft is the national-chain fill-in store. It sits on James Canyon Highway, not on the walkable Burro Avenue shopping strip, and it serves a different function from any of the independent boutiques in the guide: this is the place you stop when you realize at check-in that you forgot trash bags, or when the kids need snacks for the drive home. It's useful; it's not editorial.

For visitors passing through on a one-day trip, Family Dollar is rarely worth a stop. For three-night stays, it's likely the answer to at least one small problem. For locals, it's a routine weekly call.
