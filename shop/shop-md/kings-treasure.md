# King's Treasure Thrift Store

## Overview

King's Treasure Thrift Store is better understood as a mission-linked thrift store than as a standard gift shop. Its own website makes clear that the Cloudcroft location is one of two stores operated under Southern New Mexico Outreach and that proceeds help support Pregnancy Resources Plus in Alamogordo. That mission connection is the key fact that separates it from many other retail stops in town.

## Confirmed facts

- **Business name:** King's Treasure Thrift Store
- **Address:** 202 Burro Ave, Cloudcroft, NM 88317
- **Phone:** 575-682-5524
- **Website:** https://www.kingstreasure.org/
- **Mission link:** proceeds support Pregnancy Resources Plus of Alamogordo
- **Type of shop:** thrift store / secondhand household and clothing store
- **Status:** appears current

## What the store appears to be

The store's own site says King's Treasure offers clothing, home decor, jewelry, furniture, kitchenware, and more. It also says the Cloudcroft store accepts donations. That makes this a practical browse for secondhand goods and small-town thrift finds, not a curated boutique experience.

For readers, the most useful framing is simple: if you want resale inventory, mixed household goods, and a mission-driven shop, this is worth a stop. If you want tightly curated fashion, handmade art, or specialty local products, this is not that kind of store.

## Best for

- thrift shoppers
- bargain hunters
- shoppers looking for household odds and ends
- people who like resale inventory that changes often
- visitors who prefer spending at a mission-linked operation

## Practical notes

The store website is much stronger than third-party directory pages for this business. In this case, I would trust the official site over older directory listings, especially on phone number and hours.

The site currently lists the Cloudcroft store at **Mon-Sat 10am-5pm**. That is useful, but as with any small-town retail operation, it is still worth checking before a special trip.

## What I would not overstate

- exact day-to-day furniture inventory
- the volume of donations or turnover
- whether pickup or delivery services offered in Alamogordo are equally available from the Cloudcroft store
- exact email routing for the Cloudcroft location beyond what the site clearly ties to the organization
