# Laughing Leaf

## Quick Summary

Laughing Leaf is a cannabis dispensary at 504 Burro Ave. in Cloudcroft, New Mexico. It serves adults 21 and older and is one of the village’s locally owned cannabis shops. The Cloudcroft store is on the boardwalk, across from The Turquoise Shop, which makes it easy to include on a walk through the village’s main shopping area.

The shop describes its focus as curated cannabis products, customer guidance, and education. For visitors who are new to dispensaries, this matters: Laughing Leaf told the Cloudcroft Reader that many of its tourist customers are first-time dispensary visitors, and that the staff emphasizes customer service and education.

Laughing Leaf also operates a second location in Tularosa. This page focuses on the Cloudcroft store.

## What You’ll Find

Public sources identify Laughing Leaf as a medical and recreational cannabis dispensary. Product selection changes, so confirm current inventory with the shop before visiting.

Likely shopping categories include:

- Cannabis flower
- Pre-rolls
- Edibles
- Vape cartridges and related cannabis products
- Cannabis concentrates
- Adult-use recreational cannabis products
- Medical cannabis products
- Staff guidance for customers with product questions

## Why Visit

Laughing Leaf is a convenient stop for adults 21 and older who are already shopping or walking along Burro Avenue in Cloudcroft. The store’s location on the boardwalk puts it in the middle of the village’s visitor district.

The shop may be especially useful for first-time dispensary visitors because the business emphasizes staff guidance and education. The Cloudcroft Reader reported that Laughing Leaf’s regional manager said a large share of its tourist customers had never visited a dispensary before.

## Age and Legal Notes

Laughing Leaf’s website requires visitors to verify that they are over 21 before entering the site. New Mexico allows adult-use cannabis sales through licensed retailers, but buyers remain responsible for following state law and applicable travel rules.

## Visitor Tips

- Bring a valid government-issued ID. The shop serves adults 21 and older.
- Confirm hours before going, especially in winter weather.
- Ask staff for current product availability because inventory can change.
- If you are new to dispensaries, ask basic questions about product type, strength, serving size, and timing.
- Do not consume before driving.
- Do not carry cannabis across state lines.
- Pair the stop with a walk along Burro Avenue and nearby Cloudcroft shops.

## Background Notes

The Cloudcroft Reader reported that Laughing Leaf opened on Burro Avenue in July 2022 after New Mexico legalized recreational cannabis sales. The same article reported that Laughing Leaf had two stores, one in Cloudcroft and one in Tularosa, and described both as locally owned and operated.

In that article, Laughing Leaf’s regional manager, Justin Walkinshaw, estimated that the Cloudcroft customer mix averaged about 60% tourists and 40% locals, varying by season. He also said the shop sees many first-time dispensary visitors and trains staff to focus on customer service and education.
