# New Village Hardware

## 1. Executive summary

New Village Hardware is Cloudcroft's practical hardware store — a true small-town hardware shop built around usefulness rather than browsing. The Chamber's description frames the role cleanly: this is the place that tries to "keep you on the mountain" so you don't have to drive down to Alamogordo every time a pipe fitting fails or a paint can runs out. Core categories are plumbing, electrical, paint, wood pellets, and general hardware. Best current location across listing sources is 61 Curlew Place, off the walkable Burro Avenue shopping strip. Hours consistently listed as Mon–Sat 7 AM – 5 PM, Sun 10 AM – 4 PM — call before visiting for same-day accuracy.

## 4. What the store is

New Village Hardware is a working hardware store in a mountain town. The Chamber's language is the cleanest framing: the store exists to keep residents and cabin owners from having to drive down to Alamogordo for a $4 fitting. That function is genuinely important in Cloudcroft — plumbing at altitude in winter, electrical on a cabin circuit, a paint run for an Airbnb touch-up, or wood pellets for a stove run. The store has no editorial pretension. It solves problems.

This isn't a shop for visitors who want a browse — it's a shop for visitors with a project. And for locals, it's one of the most consistently used retail addresses on the mountain.

## 6. What I'd buy

Three defensible picks from confirmed core categories:

1. **Plumbing or electrical parts.** The classic mountain-cabin save — a fitting, a fuse, a washer — that avoids a two-hour round trip down the hill.
2. **Paint or paint accessories.** A pint, a brush, a roller. Good for rental-property touch-ups and small DIY jobs.
3. **Wood pellets.** The winter-stove staple; a supply run that resets a cold-weekend schedule.
