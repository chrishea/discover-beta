# Off the Beaten Path

## 1. Executive summary

Off the Beaten Path is Cloudcroft's most established curated art-and-gift gallery — a pink-and-turquoise side-street fixture at 100 Glorietta Ave with roots to 1996. The shop's own site and the Chamber page agree on the framing: eclectic gifts plus original artwork from local, New Mexico, and across-the-country artists. It reads less like a souvenir store and more like a gallery with a gift-shop layer attached. Current notice on the official site: closed for April while work is being done — so check dates before planning a trip. Once reopened, expect rotating handmade-led inventory that can include sculpture, ceramics, metalwork, paintings, and one-off gift pieces.

## 4. What the shop is

Off the Beaten Path is one of Cloudcroft's longest-running curated retail stops. The site and the Chamber both position it as a handmade-art-forward business rather than a souvenir stop. The 1996 founding date matters — this is a shop with thirty years of curation behind the door, and the staff have actively chosen what's on the walls rather than letting distributor catalogs do the work.

Inventory rotates. Cloudcroft Reader and the site's own language both indicate a mix that can include sculpture, ceramics, metalwork, paintings, and distinctive one-off gift items. The artist roster is broader than local-only; the shop explicitly brings in work from New Mexico and across the United States.

## 6. What I'd buy

Three defensible picks using confirmed categories:

1. **A piece of original art.** The headline category — painting, sculpture, or ceramic. This is the Cloudcroft stop where you walk out with something framed.
2. **A handmade one-off gift.** The eclectic-gift layer is the shop's other half; an item with maker provenance rather than distributor provenance.
3. **A smaller ceramic or metalwork piece.** The scale-down from a wall-art purchase — gift-portable, still from a real artist.

## 7. Atmosphere and shopping experience

Off Burro on a quiet side street — the pink-and-turquoise building is a landmark in its own right. Browsing is slow, gallery-style, with owners and staff willing to talk about the artists on the walls.

## 9. Background and history

Off the Beaten Path opened in 1996 per its own site. It's one of the more durable retail stops in the Cloudcroft map — most non-hardware, non-grocery businesses in town don't hold their identity this long.
