# King's Treasure Thrift Store

## 1. Executive summary

King's Treasure Thrift Store appears current in Cloudcroft and operates as the mountain branch of a two-location Southern New Mexico thrift operation. Its sales support Pregnancy Resources Plus in Alamogordo, which makes it distinct from a standard for-profit gift or resale shop. It looks most useful for shoppers browsing secondhand clothing, home goods, furniture, jewelry, kitchenware, and mixed thrift inventory rather than curated boutique goods.
