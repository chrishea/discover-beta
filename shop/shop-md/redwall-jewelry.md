# RedWall Jewelry

## 1. Executive summary

RedWall Jewelry is Cloudcroft's dedicated handmade-jewelry specialty store — a Burro Street Exchange tenant at 505 Burro Ave, Suite 107, locally owned and established in Cloudcroft since the 1970s. The shop focuses on unique handmade pieces across a range of materials (sterling silver, gold, opal, turquoise, other gemstones) and price points. It's the cleanest specialty identity on the avenue's jewelry cluster: not a mixed souvenir shop, not a Southwest general store — just jewelry, with a Cloudcroft-rooted, nature-inspired angle. An online shop exists but explicitly reflects "a taste" of what's carried in person.

## 4. What the shop is

RedWall is the Burro Avenue jewelry stop with the cleanest specialty focus. The official site leads with handmade unique pieces in every price range and highlights the material mix (silver, gold, opal, turquoise, gemstone). The 1970s origin is unusual for Cloudcroft retail — it makes RedWall one of the longest-tenured businesses on the avenue.

Inside the Burro Street Exchange, the shop sits near Burro Street Trading Post and The Turquoise Shop. The three together form Cloudcroft's jewelry cluster, with RedWall positioned as the handmade/nature-inspired layer — less Southwest-themed than Trading Post, less four-decade-authentic-turquoise than The Turquoise Shop, more a handmade-forward boutique in tight focus.
