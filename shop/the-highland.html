Practical notes

Hours

Open seven days — Monday through Saturday 10 AM to 6 PM, Sunday 10 AM to 5 PM. The "Open now" pill at the top of the page reflects today's status.

Hunting & fishing licenses

The Highland sells New Mexico hunting and fishing licenses on-site — a practical layer most Cloudcroft retail doesn't cover. If your trip includes a fishing day or a hunting season, this is the licensing stop on the mountain.

Building & the Highway-82 stop

206 James Canyon Hwy is on Highway 82 — a destination stop, not a Burro Avenue stroll. The shop building itself is part of the appeal: beautiful, expansive, and built to hold a wide array of merchandise without crowding.

Parking, payment & accessibility

Parking on-site along JCH. Card and cash standard. Specific accessibility details for 206 JCH aren't documented; confirm before relying on it.
