What's in the shop

The shopping guide's own language: "the widest product spread in a single store." Expect NM-made goods at the front, pantry + home + boots + outdoor + clothing + gifts across the rest. Specific items rotate.

1

NM-made goods

Core identity

The cleanest signal of what The Highland is — products sourced from New Mexico makers.

  • NM-made craft & home objects
  • Locally rooted curation
  • Contemporary-leaning styling

If you want to carry home something genuinely New Mexican, start in this corner.

2

Pantry & provisions

Rare category

The pantry corner — a category most Cloudcroft shops don't touch.

  • Specialty pantry items
  • Regional food & drink
  • Gift-quality provisions

A jar or bottle from this corner travels well as a gift — and it's the most genuinely distinctive pickup at the shop.

3

Home & clothing

Home goods, décor, contemporary clothing and accessories — the lifestyle layer.

  • Home goods
  • Décor
  • Clothing & accessories

Browse widely here — the mix rotates, and the "contemporary" framing shows up most strongly.

4

Boots & outdoor

Boots and outdoor gear as a distinct retail angle — an alternative to High Altitude's pure-outfitter frame.

  • Boots
  • Outdoor gear (curated mix)
  • Mountain-lifestyle goods

If you want a boot or layer through a lifestyle-retail lens rather than a pure outfitter, this is the alternative stop.

What I won't overstate

Pair with a Burro Ave browse
