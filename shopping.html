Burro Avenue & Beyond

Cloudcroft's charming downtown offers an eclectic mix of gift shops, galleries, boutiques, and specialty stores — most within walking distance along the historic Burro Avenue boardwalk.

The Burro Street Exchange

At the heart of Cloudcroft shopping sits the Burro Street Exchange, a collection of retail shops, eateries, and specialty stores reflecting the “Old West” ambiance of the village. Stroll the wooden boardwalk and discover everything from fine jewelry and artwork to gourmet tea and handmade chocolates.
