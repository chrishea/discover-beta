About

A true bed and breakfast

The Crofting is a true bed and breakfast. The house dates to 1909, making it one of the oldest structures in Cloudcroft. It sits on Swallow Place, a quiet side street a short walk from the village’s main drag, surrounded by spruce trees.

Each room is unique. Vintage furnishings, antique decor, and the character of a 1909 house.

The inn has 8 individually furnished rooms with private balconies in select rooms and wood floors throughout. Mornings include a full home-cooked breakfast served at 8:30 a.m.

This is a stay built around charm, breakfast, quiet, and host interaction. If you want a small B&B with a personal feel and a real breakfast, this is one of the strongest options in Cloudcroft. If you want a front desk, flexible check-in, or resort amenities, look elsewhere.

Before you book

What to verify

A small B&B in a 1909 house. A quick call clears up everything.

Your room

Ask which room you’re getting, its bed configuration, and whether it has a TV or air conditioning.

Breakfast

Served at 8:30 a.m. with no flexibility. Ask about dietary accommodations at booking. Early departures miss it.

Pets and access

Pets are not allowed. Service animals are exempt. The 1909 building has stairs throughout; ask if mobility matters.

Arrival and payment

Contact the property 24 hours before arrival for your access code. No after-hours check-in. A 3% credit card surcharge applies.

Location

Quiet but close to everything

Two blocks from Burro Avenue. A quiet residential street, walkable to shops and restaurants.

Walkable

Cloudcroft village center: less than 0.5 miles. Sacramento Mountains Museum: walking distance. Lincoln National Forest trailheads: immediate vicinity.

Short drives

Cloudcroft Ski Area: 3 miles. Grindstone Lake: nearby.

Day trips

White Sands National Park: about 35 miles west. NM Museum of Space History (Alamogordo): about 20 miles.

Good to know

Elevation 8,676 feet. No AC in rooms; not needed at altitude. Free self-parking on-site. Smoke-free property.

Pets not allowed. Service animals exempt.

Children and extras

Children welcome. No rollaway beds or cribs. The 1909 building has stairs throughout; ask if mobility matters.
