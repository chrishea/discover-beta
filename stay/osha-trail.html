About

Apartment-Style, Walkable, and New

Osha Trail Lodging is a small apartment-style short-term rental complex on Mexican Avenue in central Cloudcroft. It is not a hotel. It is a multi-unit complex of entire rental apartments booked through Airbnb, with mid-century-modern interiors, full-sized kitchens, new appliances, and a host (Homee LLC) flagged as a Superhost on the platform.

The strongest selling point is location. The property sits a short walk from Burro Avenue shops, restaurants, the local brewery, and forest trailheads. Airbnb describes Unit 1 as “steps from Burro Street in downtown Cloudcroft.”

If you want apartment space, a kitchen, and self check-in close to town, Osha Trail Lodging is one of the strongest options in the village. If you want front-desk service, daily housekeeping, or direct phone booking, it is not the right fit.

Is It Right for You?

Who Should Book Osha Trail Lodging

A short-term rental for travelers who want apartment space, a kitchen, and walkable village access — not full hotel services.

Best For

  • Visitors who want to walk to Burro Avenue
  • Couples or small families who want apartment-style space
  • Guests who want a full kitchen instead of a hotel room
  • Hikers and casual outdoor visitors who want quick access to town and trailheads
  • Travelers comfortable with self check-in
  • Dog owners who accept the per-pet fee and the no-cat policy

Consider Alternatives If…

  • You want a front desk, room service, or daily housekeeping
  • You need direct phone booking instead of Airbnb
  • You dislike strict no-refund cancellation rules
  • You travel with cats — they’re not allowed
  • You need abundant on-site parking for a large vehicle or trailer
  • You prefer to avoid a building that has a cannabis dispensary in a separate lower section of the complex
Guest Reviews

What Guests Say

4.97 / 5 Unit 1 · 270 Airbnb reviews

What Works

Recurring positives across Airbnb review summaries: location and walkability, cleanliness, comfort, communication with the host, accuracy of listings, and the mountain view from Unit 1. Unit 1 scores 5.0 for cleanliness, accuracy, check-in, and communication. Unit 5 is also a guest favorite at 5.0 / 5 across 17 reviews.

Common Cautions

Strict cancellation policy on at least Units 1 and 4 — host does not issue refunds for cancellations and recommends Airbnb travel insurance. On-site parking is limited; guests are asked not to block road access. The complex includes a cannabis dispensary in a separate lower section; Airbnb notes it is quiet and discreet, with no smoking allowed on premises.

The Pattern

Guests who understand they are booking a self-managed short-term rental tend to like Osha Trail Lodging a lot. Guests expecting hotel-style flexibility are more likely to be mismatched. Set Airbnb expectations, not hotel expectations.

Location

Steps from Burro Avenue

Central Cloudcroft on Mexican Avenue. Quiet block, walking distance to shops, restaurants, and trailheads.

Walking Distance

Burro Avenue shops, restaurants, and the local brewery are a short walk. Cloudcroft Brewing is the nearest bar; Burro Street Trading Post and the boutique shopping core are minutes away.

Trailheads

Lincoln National Forest trailheads are within easy walking and short driving distance. Osha Trail itself is among the village’s named trail networks.

Day Trips

White Sands National Park — 35 miles west, a worthwhile day trip from the mountain. Sacramento Mountains scenery on the drive each direction.

Address

102 Mexican Ave, Cloudcroft, NM 88317. Elevation 8,676 ft. Real-estate listings identify this address; verify with the host before publishing or relying on it for permitted access.

Policies

From current Airbnb listings, especially Unit 1.

Check-in / Check-out

Check-in after 3:00 PM. Check-out by 11:00 AM. Self check-in by keypad on Units 1 and 5.

Strict on at least Units 1 and 4. The host does not issue refunds; Airbnb travel insurance is recommended.

Parking & Smoking

Free on premises (limited). No smoking allowed on premises.
