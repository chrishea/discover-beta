About

A True Small-Town B&B

Burro Street Boardinghouse is a true small-town B&B — not a hotel with breakfast tacked on. It’s a log-style house with three guest rooms, antique furniture, original art, a wood-burning stove, and an old-fashioned home-cooked breakfast served daily.

The living area has a cathedral ceiling and a wood-burning stove. A deck offers views toward downtown Cloudcroft. The property has been a Burro Avenue cornerstone for decades, operated by owner Linda Carter.

If you want a quiet, personal B&B stay within walking distance of downtown, this is one of the most charming options in Cloudcroft. If you want a front desk, resort amenities, or a standardized room experience, look elsewhere.
