About

A Hostel for Adventurers

Cloudcroft Hostel is a genuine hostel — not a hotel with a quirky name. It’s a budget-friendly, community-oriented mountain base in High Rolls Mountain Park, right on Highway 82 between Alamogordo and Cloudcroft. Trails start right outside the front door.

The strongest reasons to book here are price, the shared kitchen and common spaces, group flexibility (reserve an entire floor for up to 25 guests), and immediate access to outdoor recreation. The tradeoffs are equally clear: shared bathrooms, hostel norms, and a location that requires driving to reach Cloudcroft’s restaurants and shops.

Run by Stephanie and Andrew, this is a deliberate choice for travelers who want value, shared space, and a mountain basecamp. If you want privacy, on-site dining, or standard hotel amenities, this isn’t it.

Guest Reviews

What Guests Say

⭐

What Works

Cleanliness consistently praised. Kitchen and common areas are well-valued. Hosts and check-in experience described positively. Group and family stays work well. Mountain atmosphere is a recurring strength.

💬

Common Cautions

Shared bathrooms and hostel norms are part of the deal. Privacy is lower than an inn or motel. Location requires driving to central Cloudcroft. Comfort expectations need to match the hostel model.

💡

The Pattern

Guests who understand what a hostel is like it a lot. Guests expecting a traditional hotel may be mismatched from the start. This is a feature, not a bug.

Location

High Rolls Mountain Park

On Highway 82 between Alamogordo and Cloudcroft. Not in the village — but right on the trails.

🏕️

Trails & Outdoors

Lincoln National Forest trailheads right outside. Trestle Recreation Area and Cloudcroft Ski Area are nearby.

🚗

Drives

Cloudcroft village — short drive up the mountain. Sunspot Solar Observatory — ~20 miles. White Sands National Park — ~50 miles west.

📍

Address

1049 Hwy 82, High Rolls Mountain Park, NM 88325. Free private parking on-site.
