About

A themed family-run motel on James Canyon Highway

Dusty Boots is a small, family-run themed roadside motel where personality is the main draw. The rooms are part of the experience. 16 themed rooms run the spectrum from I Love Lucy to Harry Potter to Roy Rogers, with Gilligan’s Island and Route 66 in the mix and more being added as the owners keep evolving the property.

The on-site Dusty Boots Cafe is a real reason to consider staying here. Summer hours run 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. daily. Breakfast is served daily from 7:00 a.m. to noon — the hiker boot omelette, loaded with veggies, is a fave. More than 90% of the menu is made from scratch, and motel guests get complimentary room service. Select Saturday evenings bring a German Night dinner from 5:00 to 8:00 p.m.: schnitzel, sauerbraten, bratwurst, German meatballs, red cabbage with apples, and homemade cottage rolls.

On the property: the cafe and a candy and ice-cream shop. Off the property, you’re in Cloudcroft — Lincoln National Forest, the village core, and the rest of the Sacramento Mountains within reach.

A look around

Rooms, cafe, and grounds at Dusty Boots Motel.

Accommodations

16 themed rooms

Rooms come with either one king bed or two queen beds. All include newer memory-foam mattresses, high thread-count cotton sheets, hypoallergenic comforters, quilts, and thick cotton towels. Mini-fridges, microwaves, coffee makers, and cable TV per the tourism listing. Themes include I Love Lucy, Harry Potter, Roy Rogers, Gilligan’s Island, Route 66, and more — the owners keep adding.

On-site dining

The Dusty Boots Cafe

Summer hours: 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. daily. More than 90% made from scratch. Open to the public — expect a wait on weekend mornings.

Breakfast — 7:00 a.m. to noon

Morning menu

Made-from-scratch breakfast: biscuits and gravy, French toast, pancakes, chicken fried pork, homemade posole, and the hiker boot omelette — loaded with veggies, a fave. Motel guests get complimentary room service. Arrive early on weekends.

Lunch and dinner — noon to 9:00 p.m.

All-day menu

BLTs, patty melts, chicken strips, daily specials. A recently added taco cantina and beer menu rounds out the lunch options. The menu rotates — nearly everything is made from scratch in-house.

Select Saturdays — 5:00 to 8:00 p.m.

German Night

Schnitzel, sauerbraten, bratwurst, German meatballs, red cabbage with apples, spätzle, cucumber dill salad, German potato salad, red potatoes, sauerkraut, and homemade cottage rolls. A select-Saturdays draw for guests and locals.

For motel guests

Complimentary room service

The official site states complimentary room service to motel guests — one of the property’s small but distinctive touches.

Summer hours. Off-season cafe hours and current menu specifics — including the taco cantina and any new dining concepts — are best confirmed by phone before your visit.

Why stay here

Candy and ice-cream shop

The candy and ice-cream shop on the property is part of the family-run dining package — small, themed, and on-brand for the Dusty Boots feel.

Family-run hospitality

The owners are hands-on. The motel reads as personal, playful, and slightly eccentric in a deliberate way — a small independent property, not a chain.

Pet-friendly rooms

Designated pet-friendly rooms are available. BringFido reports dogs of any size for a per-stay fee, but pet-friendly rooms can’t be booked online — call ahead to confirm policy and book.

James Canyon Highway location

On James Canyon Highway, with easy access to Cloudcroft proper and Lincoln National Forest. The setting is the village itself, not a resort campus.

What’s nearby

From 1315 James Canyon Highway

Easy access to Cloudcroft proper and the Sacramento Mountains.

Sacramento Mountains Museum

Nearby on Highway 82. The museum and Pioneer Village tell the story of Cloudcroft and the Sacramento Mountains from settlement to the present.

Mexican Canyon Trestle

The Mexican Canyon Railroad Trestle, built in 1899, is one of southern New Mexico’s most photographed landmarks. A short distance from the motel.

Ski Cloudcroft

Nearby on Highway 82. A short drive for a full day on the slopes in winter.

White Sands National Park

A classic day trip from Cloudcroft. Early morning or sunset visits offer the best light on the gypsum dunes.
