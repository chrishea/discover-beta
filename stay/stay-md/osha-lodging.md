Osha Trail Lodging on Mexican Ave. 

More like an apartment than a traditional hotel room, these suites offer a convenient launching point for exploring the village or forest.
What Makes it Special: One of the newest lodging options in Cloudcroft beckons visitors with mid-century-modern style, full-sized kitchens and new appliances. Walk to town or to nearby trailheads right from your door. Pets welcome.
Visit the Website: AirBnB.com (https://www.airbnb.com/l/xkKRRrET)

Price: Rates are subject to the AirBnB algorithm and range from $60 to $150 per night depending on the season. Cleaning charge and pet deposit not included. Located in the heart of downtown, this newly remodeled lodging complex is only minutes away from all that Cloudcroft has to offer.
