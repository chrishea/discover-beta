# Burro Street Boardinghouse

**A three-room log-cabin B&B on Burro Avenue with king beds, private baths, home-cooked breakfast, and a short walk to downtown Cloudcroft.**

---

## The Short Version

Burro Street Boardinghouse is a true small-town B&B — not a hotel with breakfast tacked on. It's a log-style house with three guest rooms, antique furniture, original art, a wood-burning stove, and an old-fashioned home-cooked breakfast served daily.

The strongest reasons to book here are walkability to Burro Avenue, the breakfast, king beds with private baths, and a rustic, intimate atmosphere you won't find at a hotel. The tradeoffs are equally clear: only three rooms, a 2-night minimum, a reservation-request system instead of instant booking, and limited detail on the website about policies and amenities.

If you want a quiet, personal B&B stay within walking distance of downtown, this is one of the most charming options in Cloudcroft. If you want a front desk, resort amenities, or a highly standardized room experience, look elsewhere.

---

## Accommodations

The house has 3 guest rooms. All rooms have king beds and private baths. Rooms are filled with antique furniture and original art.

The living area has a cathedral ceiling and a wood-burning stove. A deck offers views toward downtown Cloudcroft.

Exact room names, dimensions, and individual amenities (TV, mini-fridge, coffee maker) are not detailed on the current site — confirm directly when booking. Room character varies because the property is small and décor-driven.
