About

A Simple Cabin Base

Spruce Cabins is a straightforward, old-school cabin court for travelers who want a simple private cabin rather than a hotel room. This is not a destination resort. It’s a practical cabin base with online-only reservations, cabins for two or four guests, and a location close enough to town to be convenient.

The strongest reasons to book here are privacy, simplicity, a cabin-like feel, and modest pricing starting at $89/night. The tradeoffs are equally clear: the official site is thin on detail, policy specifics require a phone call, and you won’t find on-site dining, a front desk, or a polished brand presentation.

If you want your own cabin and don’t need much ceremony around it, Spruce Cabins is a sensible pick. If you want amenity certainty, resort-style service, or a detailed pre-booking experience, look elsewhere.
