About

Your Mountain Home Base

The Summit Inn offers a perfect blend of mountain charm and modern comfort, located just a short stroll from downtown Cloudcroft. Whether you choose a cozy hotel room with a stone fireplace or a private cottage with a full kitchen, you will find a welcoming retreat surrounded by beautiful gardens and towering pines.

Start each morning with complimentary continental breakfast, then relax by the outdoor fire pit in the evening. The inn's peaceful gardens and BBQ area make it easy to unwind after a day of mountain exploring — all within steps of Cloudcroft's shops and restaurants.
