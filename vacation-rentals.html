Overview

Your Mountain Home Away From Home

Cloudcroft offers over 150 vacation rentals across two major platforms — VRBO and Airbnb. Whether you are looking for a cozy one-bedroom cabin for a romantic getaway or a spacious mountain home for a family reunion, you will find the perfect property among the pines at 8,676 feet.

Most rentals feature full kitchens, WiFi, and free parking. Over 70 properties welcome pets, and many include fireplaces, hot tubs, and mountain views. With average ratings of 4.8 stars on VRBO and 4.9 stars on Airbnb, Cloudcroft rentals consistently deliver memorable mountain experiences.

Booking Tips

Get the Best Stay

Insider tips to help you find and enjoy the perfect Cloudcroft rental.

📅

Book Early

Peak season (June–July, December) fills up fast. Reserve 2–3 months ahead for the best selection.

🔥

Check Heating

Mountain nights get cold year-round. Look for fireplaces, heating, and hot tubs in the listing.

📶

Verify WiFi

97% have WiFi, but cell service varies. Ask hosts about connectivity if you need to work remotely.

🛒

Stock Groceries

Stop in Alamogordo before arriving — 97% of rentals have kitchens but grocery options are limited in the village.
