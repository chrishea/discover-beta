About

A Cloudcroft Tradition

Since 1950, Western Bar & Cafe has been the heart of Cloudcroft dining. This family-owned establishment has served generations of locals and visitors with the same commitment to quality and hospitality. From our famous all-day breakfast to hand-pattied burgers and fresh-cut fries, every dish is made with care.

Whether you're fueling up for a mountain adventure or winding down after a day on the trails, our cozy cafe feels like home. Stop in for a hearty meal, a cup of fresh coffee, and the kind of warm, small-town atmosphere that keeps people coming back year after year.
