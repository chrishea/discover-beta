Season Overview

A Mountain Winter Wonderland

Winter in Cloudcroft offers something rare in the Southwest: real snow, real skiing, and real winter magic. With an average of 80 inches of annual snowfall, the Sacramento Mountains transform into a snowy paradise. Ski Apache, about an hour away, offers 55 trails across 750 acres — the southernmost skiing in the United States.

But winter isn't just about the slopes. Cozy up in a cabin with a crackling fire, enjoy hot chocolate at the Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink (December through February), or take a quiet snowshoe hike through the frozen forest. This is winter the way it should be.

Winter Activities

Things to Do This Season

From the slopes to the fireside, winter in Cloudcroft offers endless ways to embrace the season.

⛷️

Skiing & Snowboarding

Ski Apache, about an hour away, offers the southernmost skiing in the US with 55 trails and 750 acres of skiable terrain for all ability levels.

⛸️

Ice Skating

The Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink opens December through February. Skate under the stars with hot chocolate in hand.

🏔️

Snowmobiling

Explore the winter backcountry on guided snowmobile tours through Lincoln National Forest. An exhilarating way to see the snowy landscape.

🥾

Snowshoeing

Experience the quiet beauty of the winter forest on snowshoes. Trails throughout Lincoln National Forest offer peaceful exploration through snowy pines.

🛷

Snow Tubing

Family-friendly snow tubing runs offer thrills without the learning curve. Pure winter fun for all ages in the mountain snow.

🔥

Cozy Cabin Time

There's nothing like a roaring fire, a good book, and a hot drink after a day in the snow. Our cabins are made for winter relaxation.

What to Expect

Winter Weather

Real mountain winter with snow, sunshine, and crisp mountain air.

🌨️

Snow Events

Cloudcroft receives an average of 80 inches of snow annually. Significant storms can drop 6–12 inches at a time, creating a winter wonderland.

🚗

Road Conditions

Highway 82 can be icy or snow-packed in winter. Check road conditions before traveling and carry chains just in case. Drive cautiously.

☀️

Sunny Days

New Mexico's famous blue sky still shines in winter. Many days are crisp, clear, and perfect for outdoor activities between snow events.

Holiday magic and winter celebrations in the mountains.

Dec – Feb

The outdoor ice rink operates December through February, weather permitting. Skate rentals available for the whole family.

Practical Tips

Plan Your Winter Visit

Everything you need for a safe and magical winter mountain getaway.

🚗

Drive Safely

Highway 82 from Alamogordo can be icy. Check NMDOT road conditions before traveling. Carry chains, keep your tank full, and allow extra time.

🧥

Winter Gear

Pack warm layers, waterproof boots, gloves, and a heavy jacket. Temperatures can drop to 15°F at night. Bring hand warmers for outdoor activities.

🏠

Cozy Lodging

Book a cabin with a fireplace for the full winter experience. Holiday weekends fill up fast — reserve Christmas and New Year's stays well in advance.

⛷️

Ski Apache Tips

Ski Apache is about one hour from Cloudcroft. Check their snow report and conditions daily. Weekdays offer shorter lift lines and better availability.
